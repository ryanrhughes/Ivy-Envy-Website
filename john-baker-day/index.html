On July 29th 2014, Cubs back-up catcher, John Baker earned a special place in Cubs lore and Cubs fans hearts.

With the Cubs in an extra-innings tie with the Colorado Rockies, John Baker took the mound in the 16th inning. Baker came to the plate in the bottom of the inning, drawing a walk, and eventually scored the winning run on a Starlin Castro sacrifice fly.

In celebration of this unlikely turn-of-events, Ivy Envy and Danny Rockett of The Son Ranto Podcast and Bleed Cubbie Blue are hosting John Baker Day on the 2-year anniversary of the game. This is your chance to celebrate John Baker Day with John Baker, himself. Join us at Nisei Lounge in Wrigleyville from 11am-12noon on July 29th, before the 1:20pm Cubs/Mariners game at Wrigley.

The career 0.00 ERA pitcher will be on hand to talk about his experience in this memorable game. For the $25 admission fee, you will receive admission to this event, a limited edition John Baker Day t-shirt, a digital download card for a new recording of "The Ballad of John Baker", and one drink ticket. Proceeds from this event will go to the charity of John Baker's choosing, Cubs Charities.

Danny will be performing "The Ballad of John Baker" and Katie Day will perform "By the Lakeside".

We will also be attending the Cubs/Mariners game and sitting in the bleachers, which are general admission, so get your ticket to the game and join us.
