For those of us that are counting down to the day that pitchers and catchers report to Spring Training (next Monday), the Cubs made Spring baseball feel even closer by releasing the 2011 Spring Training broadcast schedule. 32 of the 34 Spring Training games are available to anyone within the reach of WGN 720am's radio waves. Don't feel left out, Jimmy and others that are unable to pick up WGN Radio. 19 games will be broadcast via internet radio on Cubs.com.

According to Cubs.com, most of those internet broadcasts will be manned by Len Kasper and Mark Gillispie, who is the radio voice of the Tennessee Smokies. I was hoping that the first game of Spring Training, which is February 27th, would be televised for the launch of the Ivy Envy Game-Chat. However, the first televised game will be March 6th, when the Cubs host the LA Dodgers.

Here is the full broadcast schedule.  Also, don't forget that we will be in Arizona, covering Spring Training from March 19th-25th.
