I'm going to look past Mark Derosa's great performance last night in San Francisco and instead focus on the accomplishments of my favorite Chicago Cub:

 

Matt Murton has continued to work through his season-long extra-base dryspell and busted out with two doubles last night.  Murton went 2-4 with 2 doubles and 2 RBI.  He also quietly raised his batting average up 36 points.  Now, I don't want to sound really pathetic, but he also had a monstrous foul-ball that went just foul.  But this thing cleared the foul pole.  We're talking upper-deck foul by a few feet.

I realize Murton is probably trade bait at this point, so I feel like I owe it to him to sing his praises while he still wears Cubbie Blue.
