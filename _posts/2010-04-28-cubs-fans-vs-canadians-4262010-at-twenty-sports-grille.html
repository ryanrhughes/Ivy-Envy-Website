We hosted our first event of 2010 on Monday at Twenty Sports Grille in Moline.  We will be doing monthly events down there this season, and we kicked off the season with a live podcast recording.  We’d like to thank everyone that came down to watch the Cubs play the Nationals with us.  I know it’s difficult to get excited about a baseball team in April, especially when that team is playing below .500 baseball.

Congratulations to everyone that won prizes.  Budweiser was on hand to do some games and give prizes away.  Sweet Cheeks Diaper Cake Company had a Cubs cake on display and gave away a gift certificate.  Jana S. won the Sweet Cheeks gift certificate, Dave R. won the Cubs umbrella, Christian S. won a Fukudome t-shirt and Tim won 4 tickets to any Peoria Chiefs game.  We also gave away two sets of Cubs tickets for the upcoming series at Wrigley with Arizona.  These were won by Sarah C. and Brian.  Though Brian was sitting behind a mic for the evening, he was a guest and eligible to win the tickets, so accusations of tampering are unwarranted.

We were unexpectedly joined by about 75 French Canadians and their enthusiasm for Montreal playoff hockey really puts our early-season Cubs enthusiasm to shame.   But if the Cubs make the playoffs this season, I guarantee that in October, Twenty will be louder and less Frenchy.

The US/Canada border is clear in this photo


Thanks again to everyone that came out.  We owe a big thank you to our sponsors for their involvement with this event.  Our next event at Twenty is on May 11th.  This will be a game-watch and we won’t be recording a podcast.  The Cubs are hosting the Florida Marlins that evening.  Mark it on your calendar and we’ll see you there.
