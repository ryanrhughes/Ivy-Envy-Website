I don't doubt Lou that much, but last night's 9th inning seemed like it could have played out a little different.  Some of the pinch-hitting decisions seemed a little odd to me.  Henry Blanco with 2 outs in the 9th with the bases loaded?  I would send up Jason Marquis before Blanco.  And you know I love Matt Murton, but I'm not sure he's the man you count on for a double.  Extra Base hits aren't really this thing this year. 

The biggest bone-head, Eric Patterson with Ronny Cedeno in a close second.
