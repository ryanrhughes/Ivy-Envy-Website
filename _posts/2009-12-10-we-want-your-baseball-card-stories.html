This weekend, we will be recording an off-topic podcast.  We will pull out our baseball card collections and see what happens.

We want to hear your baseball card stories.  Amazing trades, cards tossed aside that you wish you had back, favorite brand/years/designs.  Send us your stories and we'll put them on the air.  All you have to do is call the following phone number which is a voicemail line:  206-203-1919.  If you're not comfortable with your voice being broadcast to the world, you can e-mail the stories to me at corey@ivyenvy.com and your e-mail will be read by former St. Ambrose football play-by-play broadcaster and regular Ivy Envy guest, Matt Veto.
