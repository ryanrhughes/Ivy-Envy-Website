On this, the 253rd episode of The Ivy Envy Podcst, Andy, Corey and Kurt recap the Ivy Envy Iowa Cubs Meet-up this weekend in Des Moines. If you missed this meet-up and you'd like to hit 9 Wrigleyville bars in 9 innings of a Cubs away game, this upcoming Saturday is the Wrigleyville Pub Crawl. You can find information for it at IvyEnvy.com/Wrigleyville or email corey@ivyenvy.com

Tom Ricketts and the Cubs released a video this week that indicates they feel they've wasted enough time and plan to push forward with their Wrigley renovations plans. In a move that I don't think anyone saw coming (or believed at first), the Cubs have signed Manny Ramirez to a player/coach contract with the Iowa Cubs. We answer listener questions, hear an Internet Muscle Flex and pick a winner for this week's photo caption contest.
