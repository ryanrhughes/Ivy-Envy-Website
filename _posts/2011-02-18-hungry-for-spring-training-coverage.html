Spring Training 2011 is underway for the Cubs in Mesa, Arizona and I assume you're as ready as I am for information about what's going on with the team.  We at Ivy Envy will be providing you a week's worth of Spring Training coverage, March 19-25.  We have been mentioning this on the podcast.  Kurt and I will be packing up and driving the 1565 miles straight through.  That will be 24 hours and 13 minutes.  We'll be hitting every Cubs game while we are out there and tweeting throughout the games, posting photos on the site and we'll also be doing daily podcast episodes.  We have a few extra things lined up, and you'll see what they are when they happen.  There's nothing worse than talking something up and then it not happening.  We can't wait to get out there and share our Spring Training experiences.

But our coverage is a long 4 weeks away.  If you want coverage of Spring Training in the meantime, the only place to go is www.BoysOfSpring.com.  Boys of Spring is run by Tim Sheridan.  Tim is the PA Announer at Hohokam Stadium and his access provides some great news and photos.  Check out his blog and follow him on Twitter  @BoysOfSpring if you are as ansy about spring ball as I am.
