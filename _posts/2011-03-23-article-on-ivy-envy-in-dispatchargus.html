If you have not seen it yet, go pick up a Dispatch/Argus to see an article written by Matt Veto about this lovely blog. We would like to thank Matt for bringing some attention to not just our site, but also fan blogging and podcasting. If you aren't in the Quad Cities, check out the story.

If you are discovering Ivy Envy from this article, welcome.  We will continue our coverage of Spring Training for the next few days.  We have some great stuff coming up.  We have experienced some incredible things since we've been here in Mesa, Arizona.  You can go back through our posts to see lots of photos.  Our daily reports are provided in podcast form.  Simply click on the media player on the posts labeled as podcasts to hear our daily updates.  For up-to-the-minute updates on what we are seeing and experiencing here in Mesa, follow us on Twitter - @IvyEnvy.

Thanks again to Matt Veto for the story and welcome to anyone discovering the site from the article.  Go Cubs!
