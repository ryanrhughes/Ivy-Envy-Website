After a slew of great games recently, the Cubs returned to their unwatchable form today as they lost to the Brewers 11-2.  Many had positive expectations for this game given the play of the Cubs this past week, and Lou was finally switching up the lineup.  But Harden had one of his worst outings of the year today, surrendering seven earned runs in two innings pitched.  The offense was still fairly anemic as well.  Despite the new-look lineup, the Cubs left 12 on base.  Soriano, now batting sixth, made Lou look like he knew what he was doing when he came up to bat with the bases loaded in the first.  He then proceeded to ground out.  The only runs for the Cubs came on Milton Bradley's two-run homer.  Overall, it was a very ugly game, but the Cubs still have a chance to take 3 out of 4 from the Brewers.
