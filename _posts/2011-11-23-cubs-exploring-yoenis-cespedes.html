While Theo Epstein has made it clear the Cubs aren't real interested in spending a ton of money in the free agent, he has taken his "& Co." (Jed Hoyer) down to the Dominican Republic to check out Cuban defector Yoennis Cespedes.

The 26 year old outfielder is being inspected by many teams and he's expected to secure between $35 and $50 million. It's far too early to speculate on if the Cubs would sign Cespedes and if they did, what that means for players like Marlon Byrd, Brett Jackson and Tony Campana (sorry, I had to throw the kid a bone).

In his showcase video, Cespedes appears to be superhuman major league ready. I mentioned the video on this week's podcast, so I wanted to share it on the site.
