In this episode, we talk about Silva's last-chance effort to earn a starting rotation spot. We run over a few things that we have forgotten to mention in the last few episodes. And lastly, we play an interview that we did with Cory Valentine and Matt Seamans from The Pizza Pit, a Quad Cities Style pizza restaurant in the Quad Cities. Want to know why there is QC Style pizza in the middle of the desert? Give it a listen.
