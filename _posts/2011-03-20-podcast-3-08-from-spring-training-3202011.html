We take in our first game on this Spring Training trip and have plenty to talk about.  We talk about the Cubs walk-off win against the Giants and describe the vibe of Spring Training games.  We also talk about our experiences in the SpringTrainingExperience.com seats with autographs, foul balls, player interaction and plenty of up-and-close views.
