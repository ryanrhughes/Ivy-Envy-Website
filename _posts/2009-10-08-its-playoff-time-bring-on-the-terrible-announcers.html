It looks like Cardinals fans are cursed with Dick Stockton this year.  I listened to last night's game on the radio, so I didn't have to suffer through old Dick.  It boggles my mind that TBS brings that guy back every year.  I don't think that calling a game is a simple task, but there are surely folks out there that can, at least, remember players names.

Bob Brenly is stuck in the booth with Stockton, this year.  I have heard a lot of Cubs fans argue that Brenly is terrible, but I've listened to a lot of different broadcasts and I think Brenly is a pretty good color guy.  But that discussion is for another day.
