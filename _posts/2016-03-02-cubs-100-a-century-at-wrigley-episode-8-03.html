On this bonus episode of the podcast, Dan Campana and Rob Carroll come on to talk about their new book, Cubs 100, A Century at Wrigley.

Dan and Rob previously published Wrigley Field: 100 Stories for 100 Years. On this episode, you'll hear them talk about putting this book together and share a few of the stories from the book.

You can "like" their Facebook page - https://www.facebook.com/100WrigleyStories to get more information on their event on March 12th and pre-ordering the book.
