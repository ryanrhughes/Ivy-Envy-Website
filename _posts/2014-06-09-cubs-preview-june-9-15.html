What happened last week: Swept New York Mets 3-0, Beat Miami 2-1; 25-35, 5th place NL Central

What’s up this week: June 9-12 at Pittsburgh, June 13-15 at Philadelphia

Who’s pitching: Monday (Edwin Jackson 4-5, 4.59 ERA vs. Charlie Morton 2-7, 3.31 ERA), Tuesday (Travis Wood 5-5, 5.04 ERA vs. Francisco Liriano 1-6, 4.54 ERA), Wednesday (Jason Hammel 6-3, 2.53 ERA vs. Brandon Cumpton 1-2, 6.51 ERA) Thursday (Jeff Samardzija 2-5, 2.54 ERA vs. Edinson Volquez 3-5, 4.27 ERA), Friday (Jake Arrieta 1-1, 2.50 ERA vs. Roberto Hernandez 2-4, 4.35 ERA), Saturday (Edwin Jackson 4-5, 4.59 ERA vs. David Buchanan 1-3, 6.08 ERA), Sunday  (Travis Wood 5-5, 5.04 ERA vs. AJ Burnett 3-5, 4.41 ERA)

Who to watch for: Pittsburgh OF Andrew McCutchen is the reigning MVP and is hitting .333 with 3 HR and 5 RBI in June. The Cubs are 8th best in the NL in terms of errors committed with 39 in 59 games. Cincinnati leads the majors with only 26 errors, while Cleveland has the most at 57.

Coming and going: June 3: Activated right-handed pitcher Hector Rondon from the paternity list and selected the contract of catcher Eli Whiteside from Triple-A Iowa. Designated right-handed pitcher Jose Veras for assignment and placed catcher Welington Castillo has been placed on the 15-day disabled list (retro June 2).
