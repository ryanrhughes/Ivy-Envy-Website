Nearly unwatchable.  I do like a pitchers' duel just as much as the next guy, but good lord.  Can we get some offense?  I want to see someone be clutch.  We came up short on Soto's liner to end the Houston game on Sunday, but at least that game was fun to watch.  These past two games in this series remind me of a post I wrote last season.  The Cubs have picked up the icy bats and can't seem to put them down.  Who will be our guy tomorrow to get that offense rolling?  I think it might have to be Farney.  The Cards fans I run into at work are starting to get a little smug, and we won't see the Cardinals again until July.  Please, Farney, we need a win tomorrow.
