What happened last week: Lost to San Francisco 1-2, Lost to Milwaukee 1-2; 20-34, 5th place NL Central

What’s up this week: June 2 off, June 3-5 vs. New York Mets, June 6-8 vs. Miami

Who’s pitching: Tuesday (Zach Wheeler 2-5, 4.31 ERA vs. Jake Arrieta 1-1, 3.20 ERA), Wednesday (Daisuke Matsuzaka 2-0, 2.45 ERA vs. Edwin Jackson 3-5, 4.81 ERA), Thursday (Jacob deGrom 0-2, 2.42 ERA vs. Travis Wood 5-5, 5.15 ERA), Friday (Nathan Eovaldi 4-2, 3.26 ERA vs. (Jason Hammel 6-3, 2.78 ERA), Saturday (Randy Wolf 0-1, 4.50 ERA vs. Jeff Samardzija 1-5, 2.54 ERA), Sunday (Henderson Alvarez 2-3, 2.97 vs. Jake Arrieta 1-1, 3.20 ERA)

Who to watch for: Daisuke Matsuzaka has one career start against the Cubs. He went six innings, allowing three earned runs and four hits while taking the loss. Anthony Rizzo has five multi-homer games in his career, including two games last August.

Coming and going: May 30:  Activated reliever Wesley Wright from paternity leave. Placed reliever Hector Rondon on paternity leave. Optioned reliever Blake Parker to Triple-A, activated reliever Pedro Strop.
