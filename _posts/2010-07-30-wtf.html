I'm watching the Cubs play the Rockies right now, and the Cubs have given up 12 runs here in the 8th inning to make the score 17-2 Rockies.  What a freaking joke.  I just about reached for the remote to end my misery, and then I thought twice.  No.  I'm watching this.  It's like my penance for caring too much about this team this season.  I'm the monk flagellating himself for his sins right now.  The sin of thinking just a week ago that this team could magically turn it around and still make a run.

Old Style is running a radio ad right now emphasizing how their beer has improved so much since they brought back the kraeusening process.  In the ad, a guy walks around asking fans how they would "kraeusen" their experience at the ball park that particular day.  The answers are all like, "Put sprinklers in the bleachers," and "Have a bottomless container of nachos."  Tee-hee.  So how would I kraeusen my experience with this ball game?  Have the ground open up and swallow the bullpen.
