Since tomorrow is Opening Day 2014, this is our annual "Season Predictions" episode. Andy, Corey and Kurt go through a list of listener-submitted Cubs and Cubs player over/under predictions. All of the categories are listed at the end of this post. We'd like to hear your predictions for these. Leave them in the comments area of this post.

The Cubs announced that Carlos Villanueva will be the #5 starter until Jake Arrieta returns. Corey wrote a post earlier in the week about Javier Baez and when we might see him called up to the Cubs and how the Super Two status plays into all of this. So he explains the Super Two situation. If you haven't heard of Super Two status or it hasn't been clear to you what it is, Corey puts it into pretty simple terms.
