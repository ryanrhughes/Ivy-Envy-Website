Opening Day for Major League Baseball is this Thursday.  This will be our third season of inviting our readers and listeners to join us in our MLB Final Standings Challenge.  All you need to do is e-mail your prediction of what the standings will look like at the end of the season for every division.  If you want, you can throw in the awards winners or playoff results.

On Friday morning, I will post everyone's predictions.  We will have some sort of prize for the winner.  It's six months away, we'll come up with something between now and then.  So go ahead and e-mail your standings in to me - corey@ivyenvy.com.
