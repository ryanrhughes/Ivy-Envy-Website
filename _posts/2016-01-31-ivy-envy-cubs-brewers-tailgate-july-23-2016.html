Ivy Envy will be heading up to Wrigley North for the July 23rd Cubs @ Brewers game. This event will be replacing our bus trip to Wrigley in 2016.

Miller Park opens their parking lot 3 hours before first pitch and allows tailgating. We will have a reserved tailgating area.

Tickets must be purchased by Friday, February 12th. You can purchase yours through the Paypal button below. Your $30 purchase includes a ticket to the Cubs vs Brewers game.

While this event is replacing the Ivy Envy bus trip, we will not be renting a bus. You're responsible for your own transportation. And after the game, please transport yourself responsibly.

Tickets are no longer available for this event. It sold out.
