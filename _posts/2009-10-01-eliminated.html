The Cubs are now mathematically eliminated from the playoffs as of Tuesday, and with only a few games left, we all have to move on to other time vacuums.  We all predicted that the Cubs would go out more like a lion than a lamb this season, and we all were wrong.  Some of us picked them to win the World Series, while others picked them to only just limp into the Division Series.  Perhaps we let our hopes dictate our picks too much, but I'm sure I will do the same thing next year. 

Personally, I put more time into the Cubs this year than I have in any other.  That time spent is partially due to this blog and podcast, but I also want to have been on board the whole time when they finally do it.  Right now it is hard to imagine spending the same amount of time next year and having it end up the same way, but that's what I'll do.
