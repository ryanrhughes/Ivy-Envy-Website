On this, the 360th episode of The Ivy Envy Podcast, we recap the first two games of the 2015 NLCS.

The Cubs drop the first two games in New York to the Mets. We talk about the pitching performances by Lester and Arrieta and the disappearance of runs from the Cubs offense.
