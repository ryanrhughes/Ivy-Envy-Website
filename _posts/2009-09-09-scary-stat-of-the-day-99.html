You know, it hasn’t been difficult at all to find scary stats recently.  Today’s scary stat shows how much the Cubs just handed the division over to the Cardinals.

The last time the Cubs gained any ground on the Cardinals was August 3rd.  The Cubs beat the Reds and the Cardinals did not play.  So the Cubs gained a ½ game.  The last time the Cubs gained a full game on the Cardinals was July 30th, forty days ago.  On July 30th, the Cubs were in first place in the division and led the Cardinals by .5 games.

If a rabbit conceived on the last day the Cubs gained ground on the Cardinals, it’s babies would be a week old.
