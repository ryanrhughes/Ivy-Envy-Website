It's been no secret that since the Cubs pulled a virtual no-show at the Winter Meetings in Indianapolis, Cubs fans have been a little anxious about the lack of off-season activity.  But, once again, the Cubs show us that we have no reason to question or doubt them.  So, while the Phillies ink Roy Halladay and the Red Sox get John Lackey, the Cubs make a move to show who is truly willing to put a winning product on the field.
