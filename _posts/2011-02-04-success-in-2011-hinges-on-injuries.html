I realize that the title of this post is a bit of a cliche.  That statement is true for every team in baseball.  There are 2 or 3 players on every team that must, for the most part, stay healthy for the team to compete for their division title.  The difference between that type of situation and the 2011 Cubs is if the Cubs stay healthy, they will MAYBE compete for the division.  If they don't stay healthy, come September, they could easily be battling the Pirates to stay out of the cellar.

The Cubs are a much younger team than what we have seen for a number of years. If injuries do happen (and they will), these young and inexperienced players will be called on to step up. Over the next week or two, I will look at each position on the team and identify the weak spots in the line-up in regards to injuries and depth.
