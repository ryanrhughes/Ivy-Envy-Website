Andy, Corey, Kurt and Ryan review the miserable week in Cubs-land.  We look back at the disastrous loss to the Astros on Tuesday, when Marmol blew a save opportunity.  Bob from Moline asks us about the criticisms of Quade, which leads us into some Sandberg talk.  We also comment on the criticisms of the Ricketts family.  Jason from Moline asks about who we think should represent the Cubs in the All-Star Game this year.

Then, we welcome back John from Wasting Away in Wrigleyville to tell us about their annual Pub Crawl, which is this upcoming Saturday, June 11th.  Then he sticks around to discuss the topic given to us by Nick from Virgina about the idea of the Cubs signing Pujols.  Kurt gives us his scary stat of the week and shares his concern that he might run out of material for this segment - not because the Cubs don't have scary stats, but because he's covered every aspect of the game.  Then, to wrap things up, we read your photo caption contest entries.

On Tuesday, we will be giving two tickets to see Andre Dawson in Peoria at the Chiefs game on Saturday, June 11th.  "Like" us on Facebook or follow us on Twitter and on Tuesday, we'll announce how we will be giving those away.
