Andy, Corey and Kurt discuss many Chicago Cubs topics on the 256th episode of The Ivy Envy Podcast. We start things off by not necessarily discussing Kris Bryant's numbers and fawning over him, but talking about the timing of his promotion to AAA Iowa. We come up with a few possible explanations why it hasn't happened yet and we speculate what we believe the Cubs plan may be for Bryant.

Kyle Schwarber has signed with the Cubs and started creating a buzz in Boise. We answer a handful of listener questions, read a few Internet Muscle Flexes and pick a winner for this week's photo caption contest.
