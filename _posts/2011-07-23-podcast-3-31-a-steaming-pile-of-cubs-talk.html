Andy and Corey look ahead to the trading deadline and discuss the moves that the Cubs could make and the idea of re-building. We also continue the discussion the situations with Hendry and Quade.

Mike Quade calls out Starlin Castro and Darwin Barney for their miscommunication in the infield and we tell you whether we agree with this action or not. We touch on a few topics that were given to us by listeners and we pick a photo caption winner.

Be on the look-out for an interview with Tim Sheridan. That should be up on Wednesday and he'll be sharing information on a project he's working on.

Corey will have the opportunity to meet Tom Ricketts and participate in a media session with the Cubs' Chairman. We know you have lots of questions for Ricketts and we want to hear them.
