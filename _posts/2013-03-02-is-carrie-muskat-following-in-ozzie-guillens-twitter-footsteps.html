Most baseball fans know what an entertaining read Ozzie Guillen's tweets are. I feel like Ozzie is past his Twitter prime, but much like Jose Canseco, it doesn't take him long to escalate his presence and launch into rants and observations to leave you entertained and baffled, often in the same tweet.

The MLB season is a long one not just for the players and managers, but also the beat writers that cover the teams. On Twitter, Carrie Muskat is typically pretty straight-forward and middle-of-the-road. Nothing she puts out there will shock you. But recently, I have to admit that a few of her tweets have caught me off-guard and make me wonder if this becomes the season that Carrie lets her hair down.

Either Carrie is having some fun with her job, or covering the Cubs over the last few years has gotten to her.
