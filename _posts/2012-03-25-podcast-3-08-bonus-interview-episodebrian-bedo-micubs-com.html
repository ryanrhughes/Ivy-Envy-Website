This is the third episode in a series of bonus interview episodes. On this episode, we have an interview that Andy and I did with Brian Bedo of MiCubs.

We've had Brian on the podcast a few times and if you are interested in the Cubs organization from the ground up, MiCubs.com is a great site to be reading. They in-depth analysis of the entire minor league organization through posts on their site and podcast episodes, MiCubs keeps you up to date with everything going on in the system.

Brian Bedo is not just the founder of MiCubs.com, but he is also a sports producer for WGN radio, producing cubs, Blackhawks and Northwestern Wildcats games and post-game shows.

So here is our conversation with Brian Bedo.
