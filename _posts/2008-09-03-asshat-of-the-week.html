It's not that he gets pumped and shows his excitement--although showing emotion on the mound is enough to make pitchers hated by rival fans (see Zambrano and Marmol).  It's the damn theatrics.  The guy has a strict warmup ritual (this is so out of control) and a strikeout ritual as well.  He never steps on a foul line, and he always keeps three sticks of gum in his mouth.  Just pitch the freakin ball.  Of course baseball has always been fertile ground for superstition, but this guy takes the cake.  If Bob Howry, Carlos Zambrano, and Turk Wendell had a love child, Valverde would be it.  Finally, have a look at his irritating winning celebration.  Maybe this is just sour grapes since our closer lost the game last night, but I almost hope that the game tonight goes to a save situation for Valverde so the Cubs have the opportunity to score a boatload of runs on him. 

In other Debbie Downer news, Zambrano refused an MRI today for some reason, and Harden is also experiencing some discomfort in his throwing arm.  Thankfully we now have some backup on the pitching front due to the expanded rosters.
