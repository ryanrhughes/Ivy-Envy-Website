What happened last week: Lost to Cincinnati 1-2, Split with Washington 2-2; 36-46, 5th place NL Central

What’s up this week: June 30-July 2 at Boston, July 3 off, July 4-6 at Washington

Who’s pitching: Monday (Jake Arrieta 4-1, 2.05 ERA vs. Jake Peavy 1-6, 4.93 ERA), Tuesday (Edwin Jackson 5-8, 5.22 ERA vs. Clay Buchholz 3-4, 6.75 ERA), Wednesday (Travis Wood 7-6, 4.52 ERA vs. Brandon Workman 1-1, 3.27 ERA), Friday (Jason Hammel 7-5, 2.98 ERA vs. Tanner Roark 7-5, 2.98 ERA), Saturday (Jeff Samardzija 2-7, 2.83 ERA vs. Gio Gonzalez 5-4, 3.93 ERA), Sunday (Jake Arrieta 4-1, 2.05 ERA vs. Jordan Zimmermann 5-4, 2.95)

Who to watch for: Washington's Adam LaRoche is 17th in the Majors with a .308 batting average. Cubs first baseman Anthony Rizzo is tied for third in the National League with 17 home runs.

Coming and going: June 28 (Recalled right-handed pitcher Dallas Beeler from Triple-A), June 22 (Activated catcher Welington Castillo from the 15-day disabled list. Designated catcher Eli Whiteside for assignment.)
