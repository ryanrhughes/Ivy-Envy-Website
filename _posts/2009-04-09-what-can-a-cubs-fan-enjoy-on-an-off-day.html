I expected myself to be bored out of my mind tonight with no Cubs game.  I'm feeling pretty calm and relaxed, though.  I have kind of been on baseball/Cubs overload in the last week.  So I kick back, turn on the Brewers vs. Giants game and pray the game goes into a ridiculous amount of extra innings since the Brewers have to jump on a plane after the game and fly to Milwaukee to face the Cubs tomorrow afternoon.

This is the first Brewers broadcast I've seen on their network.  So us Cubs fans are supposed to be afraid of their fans' numbers as they "overtake" Wrigley Field and make it Miller Park South.  Their fans think this goes beyond last fall's Brewers/Amtrak promotion.  Apparently, this massive legion of fans has decided to jump off the Brewers bandwagon.  Tonight, I saw an on-air promo during the Brewers broadcast.  If you buy a large pizza, you get a free ticket to a Brewers game.

Dear Brewers fans,
Why don't you focus on defending your home turf by actually buying tickets before you go to take over Wrigley Field. I've seen you Wisconsin guys and I know you like your pizza. But you should consider actually supporting your team.

Sincerely,
Corey
