For those of you that are outside Chicago, but for some reason declared in the "Chicago market" by the powers that be, Cubs games that are televised on WCIU are very frustrating.  Last time I checked, over-the-air antennas in Bloomington, Moline and Des Moines could not pick up Chicago stations.  We discussed this on Saturday's episode of the Ivy Envy Podcast.

I decided to find a way for you all to watch tonight's game with the Giants.  It appears that I may have accomplished that goal.

I tested it on a few other games and it seems to work.  If it looks messed up at first, hang with it.  I'm guessing that it will run more smoothly if you have a fast internet connection.

Go to:  http://www.hdlounge.net/

Click on the "Channel 4" tab.

If it does not work, click on the Troubleshoot Guide.

Then just follow the directions and download the plug-in.
***Edit*** I think Troubleshooting Guide will come up prior to the gametime. Try again when the game is starting.
I just followed the prompts and it appears to be working.   Hopefully this allows you to enjoy tonight's game.
