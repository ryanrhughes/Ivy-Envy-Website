Here is an addendum to Corey's scary stat post--After tonight's demoralizing loss to the Phillies at home (is there any morale left?), the Cubs are now 19-31 against teams that have a record above .500.  The Cubs have not shown that they can beat the "big boys" this year.  This terrible record is an indication to me that the Cubs probably do not belong in the playoffs.  The chances of the Cubs doing any damage in the playoffs (and possibly even just making it) are probably equal to someone sliding down a giant ramp, flying 35 meters through the air, and landing in a wading pool.

Let's move the pool a little and have Kevin Gregg give it a try.
