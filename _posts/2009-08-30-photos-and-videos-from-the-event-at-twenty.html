We would like to thank everyone that came down to Twenty on Tuesday to watch the Cubs and hang out with us.  We had a great time and we were all flattered that 25 or 30 of you would spend the evening with us.  If you haven't heard the podcast yet, definitely check it out and let your friends that are Cubs fans know about it.  You can find it on the player on the side of the page or in the blog entry directly below this one.  I have had many people tell us it was the most entertaining podcast we have done.  So it would probably be a good one for people that haven't heard us before.

The Cubs had a rough night behind Zambrano and got shelled by the Nationals, but it seemed fitting considering how the season has gone.  Below are pictures and videos.  Click on "read more" to see everything.
