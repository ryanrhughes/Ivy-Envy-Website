We are getting a group together to see the Peoria Chiefs (Class A Cubs affiliate) play the Quad Cities River Bandits (Class A Cardinals affiliate) on Friday, May 21st at Modern Woodmen Park in Davenport, IA.

If you haven't had the opportunity to take in a game at Modern Woodmen Park, it sits on the banks of the Mississippi River and it's a great place to see a game.  That evening is "Salute to the '85 Bears" night at the ballpark.  I don't really know what they will be doing, but it is sure to involve the Super Bowl Shuffle.  They will have their Friday night fireworks after the game, so it'll be fun for the whole family.

The best part of minor league baseball is seeing the future of the franchise in an intimate setting.  Growing up, I watched Joe Girardi, Mark Grace, Rafael Palmiero and Greg Maddux play for the Chiefs.  So it's more than big names coming down for rehab stints.

Email me at corey@ivyenvy.com if you are interested in joining us.  I will see how many people we have interested and get with the Bandits to see what kind of group offer they would have for us.
