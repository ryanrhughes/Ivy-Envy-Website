The AAA All-Star Game will be on ESPN2 tonight and I'll surely be watching.  Matt Murton earned a spot on the PCL All-Star team.  Chris Robinson, the catcher for the Iowa Cubs will be representing the team.  Another name worth noting is Eric Patterson, who will be playing in the game for the Oakland AAA affiliate.
