Andy, Corey and Kurt discuss the firing of Mike Quade and reflect on his short managerial career with the Cubs.  We look at the highlights, lowlights and his future in baseball.  We look at the options the Cubs have for a manager, one of which is not Ryne Sandberg.  We consider what it would be like for Sandberg to be the manager of the St. Louis Cardinals.

We give an update on Ken, who is in Venezuela and will be keeping an eye (maybe) on Zambrano for us.  We pick a winner for the photo caption contest and talk about Cubs Convention vs. Spring Training.
