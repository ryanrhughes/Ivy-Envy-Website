Though when it ran smoothly, it was a great picture. I also read that it runs better on Internet Explorer rather than Firefox. I didn't notice a great difference between the two.

I wanted to share how I found this so that you can watch any game that you aren't able to get through your television.  Hopefully this will help you watch any Cubs game you'd like.  This could also be very useful for those afternoon games where you're stuck at work.

First, you'll want to download TVAnts. You can do that here:

http://www.myp2p.eu/softwareitem.php?softwareid=4?=software

Then, go to this site: http://www.myp2pforum.eu/mlb-baseball/

In that forum, find the thread for the current day. For instance, today's thread reads:

Baseball Today | Tuesday May 5th [Streaming]

(You will want the Streaming, not the discussion)

In that thread, you will be looking for a link next to the game you'd like to watch. Sometimes links don't show up until right before game-time. You can also go down through the thread and someone may have posted links down there.

I just discovered this yesterday and I've just told you all that I know. Let me know how it works for you.
