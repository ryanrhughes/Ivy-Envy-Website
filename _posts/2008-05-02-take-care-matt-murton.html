Matt Murton has been optioned to Iowa since Soriano is back off the DL.  If he would have stayed off the roster one more day, the Cubs would have probably won yesterdays game. 

But Matt, we will miss you.  You only got to start one game and came in as a pinch-hitter most of the time.  Granted, you only hit .214, but I appreciate those three hits you gave us.  I hope to see you in a Cubs uniform again.  Go back to Iowa, show everyone what you are made of.  It might help if you were to get an extra-base hit or two.

Just look at his face in this picture, you know he's sad to leave the Friendly Confines to go back to Des Moines.
