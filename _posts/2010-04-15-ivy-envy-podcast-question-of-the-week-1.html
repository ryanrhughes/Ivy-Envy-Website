We will be introducing a new segment on the upcoming podcast episode. We would like to interact with the listeners more and get your opinions on things. So we will send out a question on Thursday or Friday of every week via Twitter and Facebook. So follow us there and join in the discussion. We will read all responses on the podcast. You can also respond by e-mailing us at: podcast@ivyenvy.com

We wanted to keep it pretty simple for the first question and here it is:
Who is your favorite Cubs player, past or present? What did that player do to endear himself to you?
