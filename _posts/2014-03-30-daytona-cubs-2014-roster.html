The Cubs High-A affiliate, Daytona Cubs (Florida State League) have announced their tentative roster to start the 2014 season.

Four of the Cubs Baseball America Prospects Top 10 Prospects will be starting 2014 in Daytona: Albert Almora (#4), Jeimer Candelario (#8), Dan Vogelbach (#9) and Arodys Vizcaino (#10).
