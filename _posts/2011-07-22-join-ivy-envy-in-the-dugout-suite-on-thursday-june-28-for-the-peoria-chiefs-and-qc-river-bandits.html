The Cubs' Single-A affiliate, the Peoria Chiefs, will be visiting our hometown team in the Midwest League, the Quad Cities River Bandits (Cardinals affiliate), next week and you can join us in the Dugout Suite for the Thursday June 28th game.

We have a few tickets left, so get in contact with me via e-mail - corey@ivyenvy.com if you are interested. This game was specifically chosen by us to celebrate Kurt's birthday and take advantage of the Thirsty Thursday $2 beer promotion. Also, you might just walk away with a vehicle. Reynold's Ford will be giving away a used car. I haven't heard what the car will be, but I'm going to guess...a 1985 Mercury Grand Marquis.

If you're not familiar with the Dugout Suite, here are a few photos from the Bandits' website. In case you are wondering, those fans in the photos are not us. That is the Visitors dugout right next to the suite, so it's your chance to practically sit in the dugout with the Chiefs.
