I visited Principal Park in Des Moines for the first time to see the Iowa Cubs beat the Tacoma Rainiers 18-8 on May 22. The good news is Rizzo played extremely well and was 3-5 with a HR and Steve Clevenger was making a rehab appearance at DH. The bad news is Brett Jackson didn't play and Chris Volstad was the starter instead of Travis Wood. Here are a few pictures from the day.
