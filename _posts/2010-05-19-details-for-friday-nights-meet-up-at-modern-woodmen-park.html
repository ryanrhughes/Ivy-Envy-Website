Some of us from Ivy Envy will be going to Modern Woodmen Park in Davenport, IA to watch the Peoria Chiefs (Cubs Single-A affiliate) play the Quad Cities River Bandits (Cardinals Single-A affiliate) this Friday evening.

We can meet-up down on the Picnic Plaza area before the game.  That is right along the Chiefs' bullpen and it's a cool spot to watch the team warm-up.  They will have a couple of things going on that night at MWP.  They are saluting the 1985 Bears and it will involve the Super Bowl Shuffle in some way.  Also, they will have fireworks afterwards, so the kids enjoy that quite a bit.  The park in general is kid-friendly, so bring the family.

But come on down, and if you have any questions, email me: corey@ivyenvy.com

You can buy tickets here or at the box office before the game.  We are buying bleacher seats and they are general admission.
