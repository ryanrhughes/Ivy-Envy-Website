Andy, Corey and Kurt convene for the 252nd episode of the Ivy Envy Podcast.

Our Iowa Cubs Game Meet-up is this upcoming Saturday. If you'd like to meet up with us for beers before the game and join us in the suite for the game, check out the details at IvyEnvy.com/Iowa. Also, the following Saturday (May 31st) is the Wasting Away in Wrigleyville Pub Crawl. If you'd like to join us for a tour of Wrigley Field and hit 9 bars in 9 innings, you can find the details at IvyEnvy.com/Wrigleyville.

The Cubs minor league system has another no-hitter, this time from Kane County. Jorge Soler suffers another injury and we talk about his injury struggles. Edwin Jackson has put together a nice little string of starts. Kris Bryant continues to tear it up at AA Tennessee and we discuss the timing of his promotion to AAA Iowa.

We also look at the Cubs run differential in 2014. It indicates the Cubs should be very close to a .500 team, yet they are far from it. So what's the reason for this and can we expect things to level out over the course of the season.

We answer listener questions, one of which leads into Kurt's Scary Stat of the Week. After the break, we read a few Internet Muscle Flexes and pick a winner for this week's photo caption contest.
