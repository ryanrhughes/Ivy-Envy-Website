Andy and Corey record this episode in our off-season format, through Skype. This leads to much tomfoolery.

We talk about the fact that the Cubs still don't have a General Manager and our feelings about the Cardinals in the World Series. Corey apologizes to a listener, Michael, for missing his "Welcome Back Kotter" reference. Nick from Virginia asks about which player would make a decent player/manager for the Cubs and that gives Andy the opportunity to speculate on Sean Marshall's television viewing habits.

Again, our off-season episodes will come out on Tuesday evenings and be about 30 minutes in length.
