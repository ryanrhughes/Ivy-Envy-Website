The Cubs will host the season home opener on Monday and with a few series at Wrigley, we check in with Pat Nagle of Wrigleyville Nation to see what Cubs fans can expect at Wrigley Field this season, with the on-going construction.

Pat updates us on the renovations inside the ballpark, outside the ballpark and around the neighborhood. He also does this while witnessing Kyle Schwarber's injury.
