See for yourself. If only I had a video of me throwing left handed. Very awkward. His unmistakable form caught my attention in Monday's game when he made a throw to the plate. I must have played it back five times. I even got confirmation from my girlfriend that he throws kind of like a girl (sorry ladies). He's like watching a little leaguer playing for a major league team, Rookie of the Year style. He even has that short, choppy warm-up swing that every 12 year old does before they step into the box. The only thing he's missing is a few hard cracks on home plate with his bat. I'm amazed and frustrated that he's as good as he is. 

Keep an eye on him tonight, especially if he makes a throw to the plate. And, if you haven't already, try throwing left handed (if you're right handed) next time you're playing some catch.
