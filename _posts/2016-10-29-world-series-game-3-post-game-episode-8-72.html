Well, the Cubs dropped Game 3 of the 2016 World Series. It was the first World Series game at Wrigley in 71 years and although the wind was whipping out, Cleveland was able to beat the Cubs, 1-0.

On this episode, we share our thoughts on the game. We also play listener voicemail that we've received over the last few days.
