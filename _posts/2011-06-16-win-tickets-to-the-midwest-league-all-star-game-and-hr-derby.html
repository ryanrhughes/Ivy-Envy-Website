Our hometown QC River Bandits (Cardinals affiliate) are hosting the 2011 Midwest League All-Star Game festivities and we'll be giving tickets away to the All-Star Game and Homerun Derby.  Greg Rohan will represent the Peoria Chiefs for the Western Division.  Rohan was chosen because to participate in the Homerun Derby because he is leading the Midwest with a .341 batting average.  The Homerun Derby will take place at 7pm on Monday.

Seven Peoria Chiefs were selected to represent the Western Division in Tuesday night's All-Star Game.   Matt Szczur, Greg Rohan and Pierre LePage will be starting in the game.  Micah Gibbs, Richard Jones, Austin Kirk and Elliot Soto will be reserves in the game.

You can win tickets to these events by "liking" our Facebook page and on Friday morning, we'll announce how you can win.  This is a great opportunity to see the best of the Midwest League in one game.  Don't miss them when they're right in your backyard.
