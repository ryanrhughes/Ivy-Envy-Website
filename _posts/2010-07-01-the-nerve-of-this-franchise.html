In June, the Cubs went 10-16.  Their overall record is 34-45, 11 games under .500 a week and a half before the All-Star break.  Wax Paper Beer Cup (via Twitter) pointed out today that they are on track to win fewer than 70 games this season.  I could go on and on with everything wrong with this team.  But what pops into my e-mail inbox multiple times a day?  An e-mail from the Cubs encouraging begging me to vote for these guys to go to the All-Star Game.  And why should I vote for position players that are part of a team that ranked 3rd to last in the league for runs scored in June?  Yeah, I'll make sure I vote as many times as MLB allows.
