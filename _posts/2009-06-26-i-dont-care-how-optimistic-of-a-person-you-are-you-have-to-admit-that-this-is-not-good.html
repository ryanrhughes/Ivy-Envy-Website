This last series against the Tigers indicates that my last post, "Cubs return to form in Atlanta", was pretty accurate.  I missed the entire series, driving to California.  Part of me is not sure if I want to even look at the game summaries.  Andy kept me updated through text that the Cubs had continued on their losing ways.  I said that I felt the Cubs needed to go 8-5 in these two weeks.   If you look at the 8-5, you realize those are not high expectations.  If you look at the way the Cubs are playing, those are unrealistic expectations.  This team is 9-11 in the month of June.  I'm officially declaring it "June Swoon".

I don't care how optimistic you are.  Even Tim, the guy who can look at a 62-100 Cubs team and claim they are the best team in baseball has to admit that this is not looking good.  I'm not saying the season is a wash, but seriously, has this 2009 club shown you anything ?  Is there one sign that things will definitely turn around?
