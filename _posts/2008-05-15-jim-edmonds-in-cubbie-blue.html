Apparently, all Jim Edmonds needs to do is pass a physical and he'll be a member of the Chicago Cubs.  I have disliked Jim Edmonds for years.  I'm sure he's thrilled to be reunited with Jason Marquis (see below).

His highlight reel catches are a product of him being slow, he has to dive for nearly every ball hit to Center Field.  I understand the need for power out of a lefty, but I really don't think Edmonds is the way to go.  I hate to see Reed Johnson on the bench and he's the odd man out.  Well, I guess Pie is literally the odd man out as he'll be sent back to Triple A Iowa. 

I just don't feel that our lack of power out of a lefty is a massive issue right now.  The fact we don't have a full rotation of starters seems to me to be a more pressing issue.

But if Jim Edmonds wears blue pinstripes, I will root him on.  It's going to be hard for me to stomach.  Besides Barry Bonds and AJ Pierzynski, Jim Edmonds is probably my least favorite player.  He is playing so poorly that he was cut by the worst team in baseball, but apparently Jim Hendry has a plan.   Like they say, Jim "If it ain't broke, don't fix it".  We're scoring runs just fine.
