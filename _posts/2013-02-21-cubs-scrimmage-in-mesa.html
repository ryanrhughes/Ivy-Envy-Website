White Team
DeJesus CF
Castro SS
Rizzo 1B
Soriano LF
Schierholtz RF
Navarro C
Stewart 3B
Barney 2B
Hairston DH

Blue Team
Valbuena 2B
Sappelt CF
Castillo C
Nelson 1B
Soler LF
Baez SS
Lake 3B
Jackson RF
Bogusevic DH

The White team bested the Blue team, 7-3. Starlin Castro went 3-3 in the game, but the highlight of the game was a HR hit by #3 Prospect Jorge Soler. We are having trouble embedding videos on our site. Do yourself a favor and go check out the video.

It is encouraging the see one of the highly-touted prospects perform well. But what do we really learn from that hit? Soler can tattoo Nick Struck-caliber pitching.

The Cubs will have another scrimmage at Hohokam tomorrow and then open Spring Training games on Saturday vs. the Angels.
