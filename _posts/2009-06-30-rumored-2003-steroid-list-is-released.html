Since I'm on vacation, I'm a bit out of the loop.  This may have been discussed on Chicago-based and national sports radio.  It may be the biggest news in sports or it's so flimsy nobody has noticed it.  I have no idea if this is true and I'd suggest you give it the same amount of credibility.  Supposedly, the list of baseball players that tested positive for steroids in 2003 has been released.  Some of the players with connections to the Cubs are:  Nomar Garciaparra, Alfonso Soriano, Milton Bradley, Derek Lee, Cliff Floyd, Moises Alou, Corey Patterson, Carlos Zambrano, Mark Prior, Kerry Wood, Matt Clement, Antonio Alfonseca, Gary Matthews Jr and Aramis Ramirez.

If the list is accurate, some of those names don't surprise me and some shock me....even though they probably shouldn't.
