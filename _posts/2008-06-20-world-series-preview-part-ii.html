Well, if the Cubs were to get to the World Series and face the Rays, let's hope the National League pulls through in the All-Star Game and gives the Cubs home field advantage.  The Cubs have lots of travel and not very much rest ahead of them and tomorrow's 1:20 start against the South Siders. 

The Cubs are leaving Tampa bruised and battered.  The injury bug has hit them bad in the last week, Alfonso Soriano, Carlos Zambrano, Reed Johnson and Jim Edmonds.

One bright spot:  St. Louis struggled with the powerhouse of the American League...the Royals, and the boys in blue didn't lose any ground.
