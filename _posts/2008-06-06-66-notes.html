*  It's been kind of quiet here at Ivy Envy.  We try to keep things moving along and fresh for regular readers, but it's tougher to write when your team rolls off 9 straight and 10 out of 11.  I'm naturally kind of a criticizer and you can imagine that it's been pretty hard for me to find major faults with this team since they left Pittsburgh. 

*  It was nice to see Greg Maddux pitch again.  The guy has still got it.  I think he was just auditioning for a spot on the Cubs roster after the Padres admit they are out of any race.

* Yesterday was draft day and anytime I think of the Cubs drafting, I think of Earl Cunningham, the Cubs first pick in the 1989 draft.  I had the pleasure of seeing Earl play for the Peoria Chiefs in 1990.  He was ejected from the game for disputing a foul ball.  Earl played 7 seasons and never made it out of Single-A ball for the Cubs, Angels and Rockies.  What's even more impressive is that he struck out nearly half of the times he went to the plate.
