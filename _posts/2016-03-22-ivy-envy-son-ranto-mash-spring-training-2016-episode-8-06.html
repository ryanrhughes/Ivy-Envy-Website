We've got something different for you this week. We are currently in Arizona for Cubs Spring Training.

On this episode, we invite Danny Rockett and Michael from Colorado to sit in on this episode with us. These two are hosts of The Son Ranto Show, a fine Cubs podcast.

We're also joined mid-episode by podcast listener, Kris Bonner and his friend, Kyle (who happens to be a Dodgers fan).

We open up and taste a vintage 2012 Cubs wine, talk about the Spring Training experience, share a few stories and talk about our 2016 Cubs expectations.

Andy brings a few Rube Waddell stories and Corey shares some Internet Muscle Flexes.
