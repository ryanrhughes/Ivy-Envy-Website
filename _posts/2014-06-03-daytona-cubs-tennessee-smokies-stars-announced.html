The All-Star rosters have been released by the Florida State League and the Southern League.

As if there was any question, Kris Bryant is an All-Star for the Tennessee Smokies in the Southern League. In all, six players from the Smokies roster earned an All-Star spot. Kris Bryant will be joined by Stephen Bruno, John Andreoli, Corey Black, Armando Rivero. Catcher Rafael Lopez was also named an All-Star, but will not be eligible to play because of his promotion to Iowa earlier today.

The Southern League All-Star Game will take place on June 17th at AT&T Field in Chattanooga, Tennessee.

The Florida State League North Division All-Star team will be managed and coached by Daytona Cubs manager, Dave Keller and his staff. Four players from Daytona's roster will represent the North division: Gioskar Amaya, Bijan Rademacher, Andrew McKirahan and Felix Pena. The game will be on June 14th in Bradenton, Florida.
