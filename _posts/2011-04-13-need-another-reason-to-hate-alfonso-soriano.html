Here at Ivy Envy, we try to be responsible fake journalists. After I posted about the horrendous Joey Votto shirt on Monday, Brian over at Chris Sabo's Goggles quickly directed me to this shirt.

The Soriano shirt is $15 bucks cheaper than the Votto shirt. It could be because Votto was the NL MVP last year and has a bright future ahead of him.  It could be because the Votto shirt is double-sided.  Or it could be due to the fact that way more Reds fans will jump all over these t-shirts, only to promptly cut off the sleeves to look as much like Larry the Cable Guy as possible. God help me if I see a Cubs fan wear one of these Soriano shirts.
