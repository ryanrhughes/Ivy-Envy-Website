The Chattanooga Lookouts and the rest of the Southern League had better look out because here come the Smokies! The Smokies are 5-2 heading into today's double header versus the Montgomery Biscuits.

This team exists.

The Smokies' roster is loaded with future big league players, and they are playing well together at the moment.  Allow me to introduce--

 

Kevin Mattison

Unfortunately, Kevin Mattison plays for the Jacksonville Suns, a division opponent.  But the Smokies' roster features some talented players, such as Brett Jackson, Ryan Flaherty, Josh Vitters, Rebel Ridling, Jim Aducci, DJ LeMahieu, Steve Clevenger, Rafael Dolis, and Kyle Smit.  We will no doubt see some of these guys in September, if not sooner.  In fact, Corey wrote a post not too long ago about how some ridiculous fans were already clamoring to see Brett Jackson in center field.  What is crazy is that if you go to the Smokies' batting statistics page, pretty much everyone is hitting over .300.  Of course, the numbers don't mean a whole lot this early in the season, but I think it is reasonable to say that this will be a strong offensive team.  The team's potential weakness appears to be pitching if anything.  However, the Smokies have an all-around strong roster and should be a contender to win the Southern League's North Division.  It will be interesting to keep an eye on Vitters, Flaherty, Jackson, and company this season to see if they can keep the torrid offense going.
