Allow me to introduce you to the Smokies' team leaders in home runs, average, runs batted in, slugging percentage, and on-base percentage.  Oh wait, it's just one guy--Rebel Ridling.

Hey ladies--free samples of me!

I searched for some information regarding the origin of his name but was unable to find anything.  One can only assume that his parents were big fans of alliteration and poetic devices in general.  Anyway, the Smokies' 1st baseman is having a boatload of success at the plate lately.  Ridling won the Southern League's coveted "Hitter of the Week" award for his exploits at the plate.  This past week he hit .520 and had 11 RBI.  For the season, Ridling is hitting .311 with 20 HR and 78 RBI.

So what's the deal with our buddy Rebel?  Does he have any chance of playing for the Cubs any time soon?  I'd like to think so.  Ridling was drafted in the 25th round of the 2008 First-Year Player Draft.  Apparently he struggled a little early on, but after making some adjustments to his swing mechanics, he began to have plenty of success.  He hits for power, and he has a decent on-base percentage to go along with that.  Ridling's stats have my attention.  Hopefully he will continue his ascent to the Cubs.  It looks like the Cubs will try to re-sign Peña for next year, but if Ridling continues to play like he has, it will be hard to deny him an opportunity in Iowa and eventually for the Cubs.
