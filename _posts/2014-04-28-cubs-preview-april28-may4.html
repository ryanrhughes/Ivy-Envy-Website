What happened last week: Split with Arizona 2-2, Lost to Brewers 1-2. 8-16 overall, 5th place in NL Central

What’s up this week: April 28-30 at Cincinnati, May 2-4 vs. St. Louis

Who’s pitching: Monday (Jeff Samardzija 0-2, 1.53 ERA vs. Alfredo Simon 3-1, 1.30 ERA), Tuesday (Edwin Jackson 1-2, 5.02 ERA vs. Tony Cingrani 2-2, 2.52 ERA), Wednesday (Carlos Villanueva 1-5, 10.42 ERA vs. Homer Bailey 1-2, 6.15 ERA), Friday (Tyler Lyons 0-2, 4.50 ERA vs. Travis Wood 1-3, 3.52 ERA), Saturday (Adam Wainwright 4-1, 1.46 vs. TBD), Sunday (Michael Wacha 2-2, 2.10 ERA vs. Jason Hammel 4-1, 2.08 ERA

Who to watch for: St. Louis starter Adam Wainwright has faced 26 of the 30 MLB clubs in his career. His 4.28 ERA against the Cubs is the sixth-worst against any club. This week should prove well for Starlin Castro. He has a combined batting average of .281 against Cincinnati and St. Louis, with six homers and 54 RBI in his career.

Coming and going: April 24: recalled left-handed pitcher Zac Rosscup and right-handed pitcher Neil Ramirez from Triple-A Iowa. Placed Outfielder Justin Ruggiano on the 15-day disabled list and optioned right-handed pitcher Blake Parker to Triple-A. April 26: placed RHP Jose Veras on 15-day DL; recalled RHP Brian Schlitter from Triple-A Iowa.
