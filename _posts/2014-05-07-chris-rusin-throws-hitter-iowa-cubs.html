Chris Rusin was in contention for the Cubs' 5th starter spot towards the end of Spring Training until a bad outing derailed his bid. This afternoon, Rusin tossed a complete game no-hitter for the Iowa Cubs as they defeated New Orleans 3-0.

In his 9 innings pitched, Rusin struck out 3 and walked 2 against the New Orleans line-up that includes former Cubs Justin Bour and Brian Bogusevic. Rusin threw 118 pitches in the game. Before today, his longest outing on the season was 7 innings.

Rusin now has a 2-4 record and dropped his ERA from 4.71 to 3.58. Before today's game, Rusin's numbers had not looked overly impressive, but tossing a no-hitter is obviously quite an accomplishment.
