Welcome to the First Annual Ivy Envy MLB Final Standings Challenge (IEMLBFSC).  Some of us that write for Ivy Envy will be posting our predictions on what the standings will look like, come October.  We encourage you to join in with us.  Post your predictions in a comment to either this post or any of the posts of the writers' predictions.  You know baseball as well, if not better, than we do.  So why not show us up?  If a non-Ivy Envy writer wins the contest, you will be awarded whatever Ivy Envy merchandise we have at that point.  That merchandise is in the development stages and it will surely be available by then.

The IEMLBFSC will be scored in the following manner:

You will receive one point for each standing position you are off, with each team.  For instance, if you chose the top of the NL Central to be:

1. Cubs  2. Brewers  3. Cardinals

Let's say the final standings end up:

1. Cardinals 2. Brewers 3. Cubs

You would get 2 points for the Cardinals, 0 points for the Brewers and 2 points for the Cubs.   So the object is to have the lowest point total.

So list your standings for every division in baseball.  It won't count for points, but go ahead and pick your wildcard teams and how you think the NLDS, NLCS, ALDS, ALCS and World Series will pan out, too.

Deadline for the contest is noon, Monday April 6th.
