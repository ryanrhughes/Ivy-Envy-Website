First off, the audio in this episode is bad and I apologize. I didn't realize until we had recorded for an hour that the audio was being recording through the built-in speaker on the laptop, not the mics/mixer. I made this mistake once in Arizona and now I've made it again...I've learned my lesson.

Andy, Corey and Kurt describe the Ivy Envy Bus Trip to the Cubs/Giants game on Saturday. If you were there or saw it on TV, you probably know what our experience was. We also discuss Randy Wells' potential return and the bad news from Mesa about Andrew Cashner. We also talk about the Cubs' options with Tyler Colvin. We also choose a winner for the Ryan Theriot photo caption contest.

We also give a little air-time for local band/Cubs fans/Ivy Envy fans The Lion in Rome and play a song off their new demo at the end of the show.
