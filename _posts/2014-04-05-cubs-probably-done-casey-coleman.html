After being drafted in the 15th round and spending six seasons in the Cubs system, it appears the Cubs are done with RHP Casey Coleman.


#Cubs transfer old friend Casey Coleman to Class A Boise, effectively ending his time in org, reports Des Moines Register

— Bruce Miles (@BruceMiles2112) April 5, 2014

A third-generation MLB player, Coleman pitched 48 games with the Cubs between 2010 and 2012 with a 7-13 record and 5.76 ERA. Coleman was entering his fourth season at the AAA level and the move made room on the Iowa roster for Alberto Cabrera.  This move isn't completely surprising considering the Cubs have some decent arms in Iowa now, and more on the way.  There's not really room for a guy that seems stuck at the AAA level.
