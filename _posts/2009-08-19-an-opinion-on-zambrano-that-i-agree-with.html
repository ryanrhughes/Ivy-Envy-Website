It seems that ever since Carlos Zambrano admitted to being lazy, the anti-Zambrano crowd has ratcheted things up quite a bit.  Of course, many of these people are the same ones that were giddy about his no-hitter last year and putting him in the race for the Cy Young the last few years.  These are also the same people that love his excitement when he's pitching well and shun it when he's not.  Cubs fans are fickle.  They'll turn on you fast and Zambrano has been seeing this for about the last month with calls for him to be traded.

I saw Paul (you've heard him on the podcast) a few days ago, and he's ready to cut Zambrano loose, via trade or waivers.  I disagree and saw a post on another blog that I found pretty interesting.  I think it's a good read.  Check it out.
