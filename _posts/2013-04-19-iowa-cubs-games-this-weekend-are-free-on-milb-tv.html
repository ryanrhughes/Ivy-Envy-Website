This weekend, MiLB.tv is running a free trial weekend. Cubs fans will be able to watch three Iowa Cubs games:
* Friday - Nashville (Brewers) @ Iowa 7:05pm Central
* Saturday - Iowa @ Memphis (Cardinals) - 6:05pm Central
* Sunday - Iowa @ Memphis (Cardinals) - 1:30pm Central

Barret Loux (1-1, 10.50) will be taking the mound tonight against Nashville. Chris Rusin (1-1, 3.50) will pitch Saturday. Drew Carpenter (1-2, 8.10) will take the mound on Sunday. Iowa is currently sitting at the bottom of the division with a 4-9 record. Iowa is coming off a few rainouts, so you will have the opportunity to watch a fully rested Ian Stewart.

A few years ago, I subscribed to MiLB.tv and really enjoyed it. I was able to find a coupon code for it that year. I haven't been able to find one the last few years. The season package costs $40 and I believe every Iowa game is included and at times, Tennesse, Daytona and Kane County games will be broadcast.

To watch these games this weekend, go to MiLB.tv.
