When we spoke about the Matt Garza trade on this last podcast, we kind of dismissed Fernando Perez.  I happened across a few videos today that thrust Perez to the top of my "Most Liked Cubs" list.  The first video is creativity on the field that would excite any fan and would be talked about on blogs and in bars for years.
