Andy, Corey and Jeremy discuss a terrible first half of May for the Cubs, the lack of production from the middle of the line-up and Starlin Casto being showered with boo's in his first game at Wrigley Field.  We also consider what Lou Piniella's role in this is and what the future holds for him.  We question the validity of the no-hitter thrown by Nick Struck of the Peoria Chiefs.  And we read your answers from the Question of the Week.
