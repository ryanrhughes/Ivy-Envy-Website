Ivy Envy will be having it's first meet-up for Cubs fans at Twenty Sports Grille, which is located at 1405 5th Avenue in Moline, Illinois.  The Cubs are playing the Nationals that night at 7pm and we'll start things up a little before the first pitch.

We will also be recording a podcast episode, so this is your chance to see how the magic happens.   Twenty will have 16 oz. Cubs aluminum bottles for $3.50.  They also have 20 beers on tap and serve your beverage in the appropriate pint glass.

We'd like to hang out and BS about the Cubs with you.  At some point in the evening, all in attendance will be able to put their names in and we will draw for a $50 gift card to Twenty.  Come on down and bring your friends that are Cubs fans.
