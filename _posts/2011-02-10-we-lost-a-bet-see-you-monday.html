Last year, we rounded up some bloggers for other teams in the NL Central for our division preview episode on the podcast.  I didn’t know any bloggers for other teams, so I just started browsing baseball blogs.  There was one site that caught my eye purely because of the name.  Chris Sabo’s Goggles is a Cincinnati Reds blog run by Brian Welch.  Brian is a Reds fan that lives in Chicago.  It must be tough to live in the belly of the beast of the team you dislike the most.

Entering the 2010 season, we made a little wager with Chris.  The winner of the bet would be determined by whose team won the head-to-head record.  I don’t remember what the final tally was, but I’ll tell you that it wasn’t close….and the Cubs didn’t win.  I thought we had a chance to win the bet.  I thought the Cubs would be better than they were.  I looked at a pitching rotation that included Aaron Harang and Bronson Arroyo and though, “We’ll win this bet.”  Plus, I didn’t know much about this fellow named Joey Votto.

The wager was this:  The loser of the bet has to re-direct their site to the winner’s site for 3 days.  So Friday – Sunday, if you go to www.ivyenvy.com, you will see a “C” with a slightly different shape and a whole lot of red.

I should have paid up on this bet in November or December, when there was very little going on.  But I’m a procrastinator and now I’m trying to squeeze it in right before pitchers and catchers report to Spring Training.

So, farewell for a few days…. Go Cubs!
