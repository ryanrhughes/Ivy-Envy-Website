Well, ESPN can't be blamed for this, but tonight the Cubs are not on TV outside Chicagoland.  Every once in a while, when the games are only on WCIU, our local cable company will throw the game   on a public access chanel.  No luck tonight. 

It seems that this year, about once a week the Cubs have not been on TV.  Next week, Wednesday night's game against the Padres is only on WCIU.  I really miss the days that you could count on the Cubs being televised...every game...on WGN.  I don't really have anything against Comcast Chicago.  I like some of their Cubs analysis throughout the day.  But the Cubs and WGN belong together....every game.  I would think that would help with developing a fan base across the country.  TBS no longer shows Braves games.  The Cubs would be the on tv, every game in nearly every household.  They would be the only consistent baseball on television.  With the way it is now, only two...sometimes three games a week are on WGN. 

So tonight, I'll spend my evening with Pat and Ron.  "Oh maaaan".
