Thank you MLB Extra Innings.  Why in the world is the third game of the season unavailable to everyone?  So I sat outside all evening in the chilly April air to watch my Cubs.  I ended up with a small gathering of die-hards that couldn't go an evening without their Boys in Blue.

This is exactly the game I have been waiting for from Fukudome.  I don't know if he'll pan out, but like I said in the third episode of the Ivy Envy Podcast, I want him to succeed.  I like him.  4-5 with a HR, Double, Stolen Base and 4 Runs scored.  That's probably the best game I've seen from him.  I know that as Cubs fans we've become fickle, demanding perfect and winning baseball, but I'm trying to put that behind me.  I want to pull for our guys, whether they play well or not.  As long as Fukudome wears that "C" on his hat, I am pulling for the dude.  From what I saw of him tonight, he looked in control both at the plate and in the field.

I don't want to rub it in to everyone that was unable to see the game, but that offensive explosion tonight, particularly in the first few innings was thoroughly enjoyable.  I can't look past the 4 RBI apiece from Fontenot and Ramirez, but I think the night belonged to Fukudome.
