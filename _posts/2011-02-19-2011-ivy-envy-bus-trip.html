We had originally planned to do the 2011 Bus Trip on June 18th, when the New York Yankees visit Wrigley Field.  However, the marketing/ticket sales people for the Cubs kept this from happening.  If you want the whole story, check out our Podcast Episode 2.35 at around the 8 minute mark.  So we apologize for getting your hopes up.  We've learned our lesson and waited to reveal the date of the game until the tickets were secured.

[caption id="attachment_3886" align="aligncenter" width="374" caption="The Ivy Envy bus trip is officially endorsed by the Smith Brothers"][/caption]

If you have never been on a bus trip, it is a great way to take in a Cubs game.  We charter a bus and you don't have to worry about parking or anything.  The bus drops us off right at Wrigley Field on Waveland.  Your purchase price covers your bus ride up and back, and the ticket to the game.  What these bus trips are well-known for is that you are able to drink on the bus en route.  We had two guys in the back of the bus polish off a bottle of Jack Daniels before the first bathroom stop (about 90 miles into the trip and keep in mind that the bus pulled out at 8:30am) and there was gallons of Bloody Marys being passed around. We had a great group last year that enjoyed the company of other Cubs fans and it was an amazing day for a baseball game.

The date for the 2011 Bus Trip will be May 14th.  It is a very rare Saturday night game at Wrigley and the Cubs will be hosting the San Francisco Giants, who are fresh off their 2010 World Series Championship.  If the Cubs can pimp the Yankees vs. Cubs games as Jeter vs. Castro, we can pimp this bus trip as Lincecum vs Castro or Matt Cain vs. Castro.  Those match-ups are way more likely to happen, unless Joe Girardi sends Jeter to the bullpen.

I have had quite a few people asking about the purchase of tickets for the bus trip and this is how we are going to do it:  First dibs on the seats for this trip go to those that bought tickets for last year's bus trip.  If you bought tickets to the 2010 bus trip, you have the opportunity to purchase the same number of seats.  The cost for the bus ride and the baseball game will be $90 this year.  To reserve your seat, you must pay the deposit ($45) by March 10th.  On March 11th, I will announce how many seats are left on the bus and anyone can put down their deposit for a seat. I will need the remaining amount for the seats by April 10th.

If you have any questions about the trip, contact me at Corey@IvyEnvy.com
