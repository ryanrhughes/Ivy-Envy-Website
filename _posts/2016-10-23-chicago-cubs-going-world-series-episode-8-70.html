Cubs fans all over the world are experiencing something for the first time. The Chicago Cubs are going to the World Series!

On this episode, we spend a lot of time talking through some of the emotions we are feeling, the morning after. This was one of the podcast episodes we've been waiting to record, but there's an even bigger one out there, that could happen very soon.

We do eventually talk about the baseball side of Game 6 and we preview the 2016 World Series, and specifically Game 1.
