What happened last week: Lost to White Sox 0-2, Split with White Sox 1-1, Lost to Braves 0-3; 12-24, 5th Place NL Central

What’s up this week: May 12-15 at St. Louis, May 16-18 vs. Milwaukee

Who’s pitching: Monday (Travis Wood 2-4, 4.75 ERA vs. Tyler Lyons 0-2, 3.43 ERA), Tuesday (Jake Arrieta 0-0, 2.89 ERA vs. Adam Wainwright 6-2, 2.02 ERA), Wednesday (Jason Hammel 4-1, 2.45 ERA vs. Michael Wacha 2-3, 2.85 ERA), Thursday (Jeff Samardzija 0-3, 1.45 ERA vs. Lance Lynn 4-2, 3.83 ERA), Friday (Kyle Lohse 4-1, 2.75 ERA vs. Edwin Jackson 2-2, 4.57 ERA), Saturday (Matt Garza 2-3, 4.98 ERA vs. Travis Wood 2-4, 4.75 ERA), Sunday (Marco Estrada 2-1, 3.53 ERA vs. Jake Arietta 0-0, 2.89 ERA)

Who to watch for: Former Cub Matt Garza has faced his old mates only once, earning the win while striking out seven earlier this season. Junior Lake is hitting .304 with a .798 OPS in the month of May.

Coming and going: May 7: Placed right-handed pitcher Pedro Strop on the 15-day disabled list and recalled left-handed pitcher Zac Rosscup from Triple-A Iowa.
