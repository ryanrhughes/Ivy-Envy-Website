What happened last week: Lost to St. Louis 1-2, Beat Milwaukee 2-1; 15-27, 5th place NL Central

What’s up this week: May 19, off, May 20-21 vs. New York Yankees, May 22-25 at San Diego

Who’s pitching: Tuesday (Masahiro Tanaka 6-0, 2.17 ERA at Jason Hammel 4-2, 3.06 ERA), Wednesday (Chase Whitley 0-0, 0.00 ERA at Jeff Samardzija 0-4, 1.62 ERA), Thursday (Jake Arrieta 0-0, 2.70 ERA at TBD), Friday (Edwin Jackson 3-3, 3.98 ERA vs. Robbie Erlin 3-4, 4.53 ERA), Saturday (Travis Wood 3-4, 4.65 ERA vs. Donn Roach 1-0, 2.57 ERA), Sunday (Jason Hammel 4-2, 3.06 ERA vs. Ian Kennedy 2-5, 3.60 ERA)

Who to watch for: In the last 10 games Cubs OF Junior Lake is slugging .548 with 2 homers and 9 RBI. San Diego starts three switch hitters and two lefty in its everyday lineup. Left-handers are hitting .244 against Cubs pitching with 15 homers, four more than righties.

Coming and going: May 14: Activated Jose Veras and placed reliever Zac Rosscup was placed on the disabled list.
