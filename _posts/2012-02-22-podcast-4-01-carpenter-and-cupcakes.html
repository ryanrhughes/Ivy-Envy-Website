Here it is, the first episode of the 4th season of the Ivy Envy Podcast. Pitchers and catchers have reported to Spring Training the beat reporters are throwing non-stories at us left and right. Samardzija is being stretched out as a starter and Carlos Marmol was throwing a cutter last year. Also, apparently the team is so poor at fundamentals, the Cubs needed to spray paint the bases so the players knew where their feet should hit when running bases.

The one real piece of news is that the Theo Epstein compensation is done....kind of. Chris Carpenter is shipping up to Boston and believe it or not, there's a guy on Twitter that wishes the Cubs could call the whole deal off.
