With Ryne Sandberg coming to town as the manager of the Phillies this weekend, what a perfect time to pull out Bases Loaded 3 and play some baseball as my favorite player of all time!  I got this game on Ebay for the low, low price of $2.99; I bought it because I really liked the original Bases Loaded, and it had Ryno on the cover.  I paid too much.  Once you get into the game, Sandberg is nowhere to be found.  Here is the menu screen:

If he did have an avatar in the game, it would be embarrassing to his legacy because it is impossible not to make errors when trying to play defense in this game.   The way fielding is handled is madness.  There is no way to find the appropriate fielder in time to make a play because when a ball is hit, the game maintains the behind-the-pitcher point of view.  Hitting is also difficult as pitches magically speed up halfway to home plate.  If you're looking for a good 8-bit baseball game, I would go for the original Bases Loaded.  There is no way "Ryne Sandberg Plays Bases Loaded 3."  What is your favorite baseball video game of all time?
