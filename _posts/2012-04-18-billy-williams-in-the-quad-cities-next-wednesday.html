For those readers here in the Quad Cities area, Former Cub and Hall of Famer Billy Williams will be in the Quad Cities next Wednesday, April 25th. Williams will be appearing at the new Moline Menards as a part of their grand opening celebration. He is scheduled to be at Menards from 6pm-8pm.

Bears fans might be interested in knowing that Roberto Garza will be at Menards on Monday, April 23rd from 6pm-8pm and Dane Sanzenbacher will be there on Thursday, April 26th from 6pm-8pm.
