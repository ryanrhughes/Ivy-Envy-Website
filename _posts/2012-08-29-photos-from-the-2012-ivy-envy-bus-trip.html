This last Saturday, we filled a 55 passenger bus and drove to Chicago to see the Cubs host the Rockies. If you'd like to hear us talk about the bus trip, check out our Podcast Episode 4.32.

I have invited anyone on the bus to share their photos with us and our Facebook page. You can look at all those photos here. If you were on the bus and would like to share your photos, email them to corey@ivyenvy.com.

I see no reason that we will not be doing the 4th Annual Ivy Envy Bus Trip in 2013. Stay tuned to our site and Facebook page for information. I usually book the date in January or February.
