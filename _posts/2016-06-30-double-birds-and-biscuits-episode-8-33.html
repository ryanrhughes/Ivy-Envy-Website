Welcome to the 411th episode of The Ivy Envy Podcast. In this episode, we recap the Cubs sweep over the Reds in Cincinnati. Corey was at the last two games of the series and talks about the games from an inside-the-park perspective.

This was quite an eventful series and it was something the Cubs and fans needed after going 1-6 in the previous week.
