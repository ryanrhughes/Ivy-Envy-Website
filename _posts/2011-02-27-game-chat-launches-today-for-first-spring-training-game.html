A new addition to our site this year is our game-chat.  We are launching the chat today for the first Cubs Spring Training game.  The Cubs will be taking on the Oakland A's at 2pm.  Unfortunately the game is not on tv (explain that decision, WGN and CSN), but it will be broadcast on WGN Radio. We thought that a game-chat might be a nice little place for fans to congregate while watching and listening to the Cubs.  A large part of being a Cubs fan is the community.

We have mentioned this on the podcast, but interest in Spring Training games is kind of half-hearted, so if you come into the chat for a game in the next month and it's dead, don't write it off.  We expect the chat to kick into full gear on on April 1st.

You can enter the chat by clicking on "Game Chat" at the top of the page, below the Ivy Envy header.  So come on in and join the community.
