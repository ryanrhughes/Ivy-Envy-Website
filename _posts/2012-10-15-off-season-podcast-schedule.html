Our live broadcasts will continue throughout the off-season. Like in previous years, we are moving our off-season podcast recordings to Tuesday nights at 8:30pm Central. These episodes will be shorter (30-45 minutes) and we will be continuing the photo caption segment. Kurt will be putting his Scary Stat of the Week on hiatus in the off-season. We are replacing that segment with a yet-unnamed segment. You can hear the first installment in Podcast Episode 4.37. We would appreciate any name suggestions you have for the segment.

So starting this week, go to ivyenvy.com/live on Tuesday nights at 8:30pm Central to watch our live video broadcasts.
