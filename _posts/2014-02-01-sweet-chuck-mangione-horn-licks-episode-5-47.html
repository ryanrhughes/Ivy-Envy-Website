We continue to avoid Cubs talk by giving our Super Bowl predictions.

The Cubs are still held up with their Wrigley renovations by the rooftops and we spend some time talking about that. Admittedly, we are not the experts on this topic. Because of our distance from Wrigley and the fact that outsiders can't make sense of Chicago politics...and our lack of knowledge on legal issues, we engage in a very surface discussion on this topic. If this is a topic that interests you, Bleacher Nation is doing a good job of keeping up with the day-to-day developments and Wrigleyville Nation Podcast is a group of guys that live in Wrigleyville and are season ticket holders.

The Jeff Samardzija trade rumors involved the Blue Jays this week and we look at the status of his 2014 contract. If you are interested in learning more about the arbitration process, I reference this Fangraphs Podcast episode.

The Cubs added two pitchers on Friday, signing Jason Hammel, who will fit into the starting rotation. The Cubs also signed avid fisherman James McDonald.
