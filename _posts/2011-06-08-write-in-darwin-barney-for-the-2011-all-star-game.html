On last weekend's podcast episode, Jason from Moline asked us about our choice for the Cubs representative(s) for the 2011 MLB All-Star Game. Over the last few weeks, I've been advocating for Darwin Barney and it's time for the campaign to begin.

I'm really not going to get into his accomplishments. I'm going to simply sell him on you by saying this: Which player on the Cubs roster this deserves an All-Star appearance more than Darwin Barney? He entered Spring Training expected to start the season in the minor leagues. He worked hard and earned a spot on the team. Then his performance throughout Spring Training earned him the starting spot at 2nd base. And now he's become, perhaps the most reliable batter on this team.

I'm not a fan of fan voting for the All-Star Game, but I will be casting a ballot (or the 25 allowed by MLB) for Darwin Barney. It takes a little more effort because you have to write him in, but Darwin deserves a few seconds of your time. Do the right thing.
