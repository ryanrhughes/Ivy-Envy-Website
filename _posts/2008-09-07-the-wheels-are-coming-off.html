Well, a loss like this is enough to bring be out of my nearly month-long Ivy Envy hibernation. 

Folks, THAT is how the Cubs lose a ball game.  That is the most heartbreaking way to lose a 4-3 game.  Chopper up the middle for a double play to end the game?  No way.  If I needed to explain to people  the history of the Chicago Cubs, and I only had 5 minutes to explain our losing ways, I would show them the last 5 minutes of this game.  I would also explain how bad the Reds are and that they have nothing to gain with a loss other than to stick it to the boys in blue. 

If you read this looking for some in-depth analysis, forget it.  I have been away from Ivy Envy too long.  I promise to write something worth-while at some point.  The worst thing is that I knew this game was going to fall apart the way that it did.  I started writing this post before the game was even over.
