We have another bonus episode for you, this Opening Week of the 2013 Major League Baseball Season. This episode contains an interview with Wayne Randazzo of the Kane County Cougars.

Wayne is the play-by-play broadcaster for the Cougars and give us a little history of baseball in Kane County before talking about the Cougars' first season as the Class A Midwest League affiliate of the Chicago Cubs.

Wayne highlights the notable players on the Kane County roster and introduces us to the coaching staff.
