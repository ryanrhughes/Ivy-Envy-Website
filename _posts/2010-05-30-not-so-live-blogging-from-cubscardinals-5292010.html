If you attempted to follow the live blogging for yesterday's game, you noticed that:

1. If you get up early enough on a Saturday morning, time plays tricks on you and you you head out the door 5 hours before you wake up.

2. Live-blogging didn't work. I don't know care why.  But since we dedicated ourselves to it, I'll post it now.  If you wonder what the "-C" means, we had decided to be clear who was tweeting what, but we weren't real consistent with it.

Part of the purpose of this trip was for both of us to experience the Wrigley bleachers for the first time.  There has been discussion on blogs/Twitter about the conduct of fans in the bleachers and rather than blindly pass judgment, we decided that it is our duty as bloggers (a "profession" that requires no dedication to truth or fact) to experience this location first-hand.  We may discuss our findings in a post or two, but for  complete wrap-up, check out the next Ivy Envy Podcast episode.  This episode will be different than the usual episodes.  It will be a "backyard episode" in the vein of Podcast #11.  If you haven't heard that episode, it's worth listening to.

It's going to be a warm Midwestern Memorial Day BBQ with nice cold beer on tap and the Cubs and Pirates on the television.  That is a recipe for podcasting gold.  We are going to open this episode to anyone that would like to be a guest and participate.  If you're in the Quad Cities with no plans for Memorial day, get ahold of us to join us in person.
