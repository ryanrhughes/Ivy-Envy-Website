We are happy to announce that beginning the first game of Spring Training, we will host a game-chat on our site.  Are you tired of sending texts to your friends that are Cubs fans during the game?  Or do your non-Cubs fan friends get annoyed with the clogging of your Twitter feed with your complaints about Soriano's hustle or Fukudome's corkscrew swing?

Well, now you will be able to join other Cubs fans every game this season in our chat.  There won't be a registration required.  You won't need to worry about going through a process.  It will be easy, and the perfect place for you to share all of your opinions on the Cubs.  We know you have them.

The chat will launch Sunday February 27th for the Cubs vs. Oakland game.
