Before Thursday night's Peoria Chiefs vs. Quad Cities River Bandits game at Modern Woodmen Park in Davenport, Iowa, I had the pleasure of meeting up with Craig Wieczorkiewicz, otherwise known as The Midwest League Traveler. Craig is on a summer-long mission of going to all 16 ballparks in the Midwest League. You can read all of his descriptions of the ballparks and the games he witnesses at www.mwltraveler.com or you can follow him on Twitter @MWLtraveler.
