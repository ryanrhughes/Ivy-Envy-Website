Andy, Corey and Kurt discuss the current state of the 2010 Cubs and the choices management faces. Whisperings of interest from the Angels in Derrek Lee is forcing us to consider the idea of the Cubs losing players we have grown to love. Kurt gives us a little review of the new ballpark in Minneapolis and we look at the idea of instant replay in baseball with the Question of the Week.
