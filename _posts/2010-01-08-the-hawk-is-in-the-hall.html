We are a little late with this, but we would still like to express our satisfaction that Andre Dawson was elected into the Hall of Fame.  I have a weak opposition to the Hall of Fame, which I briefly explain in the 25th episode of the Ivy Envy Podcast.  But I'm happy to see Dawson get in.  In honor of his induction, I will hang a poster of Dawson that is framed in glass in the backyard pub, which is where the majority of the Ivy Envy Podcasts are recorded.  I've had this since 1988 and I am happy to finally give it a proper place to hang.

Personally, I don't care if Dawson goes in as a member of the Expos or the Cubs.  We have fond memories of him and I'm sure there are a few people in Montreal that ever knew who Dawson was.  I will always remember him in blue pinstripes with a red "C" on his hat.  I doubt that Dawson gets in without those six years he spent on the Northside and that's good enough for me.
