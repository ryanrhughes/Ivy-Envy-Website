Andy, Corey and Kurt review the best and worst of the 2012 Cubs in this episode. We each share our top 3 best and worst things about the year for the Cubs. We found it to be much easier to come up with 9 of the "worsts", but you'll be surprised that there actually were some things that happened with the 2012 Cubs that pleased us.

This list is in no way the definitive list. In fact, we'd love to hear your opinions and you best/worst list. Leave them in a comment here on the site or on our Facebook page under the post for this podcast episode.
