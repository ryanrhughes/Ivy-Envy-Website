Today is the 2012 Season Opening Day for the Boise Hawks and the rest of the Northwest League. The Hawks start out on the road against Salem-Keizer (Giants). The Northwest League is Class A - Short Season and runs through September 1st.

Mark Johnson returns as the manager of Boise. He led the Hawks to the playoffs last year, for the first time since 2006. Joining Johnson as the hitting coach is Bill Buckner. Buckner was with the Cubs from 1977-1984. He won the NL Batting Crown in 1980 and was an All-Star in 1981. Buckner hit .289 over his 22-year career.
