Though the only news I'd really like to hear from the Cubs right now would be a trade sending Milton Bradley out of town, I was happy to hear that Ryne Sandberg will be managing the Iowa Cubs next year.  Des Moines is only about 2 1/2 hours from Ivy Envy headquarters and I expect to make at least a few trips to watch the Iowa Cubs play.

After moving up a class in the minor league system every year, it would appear as though the Cubs are grooming Sandberg to take over for Piniella at the end of this year.  However, it seems like at least a possibility that Piniella will return to the Cubs after his current contract expires.

So, those of you that have not been able to pry yourself away from the Cubs and are out wandering the Cubs blogosphere, let me pose a question to you.  It's completely hypothetical, but I'm interested to hear what you people think.  Or I'm at least curious if anyone is reading about the Cubs in December.  Would you rather have Ryne Sandberg or Joe Girardi managing the Cubs in 2011?
