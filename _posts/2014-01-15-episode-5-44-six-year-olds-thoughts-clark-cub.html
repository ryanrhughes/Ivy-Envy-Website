Earlier this week, the Cubs introduced Clark the Cub. Twitter freaked out and Deadspin grossed us out. We will surely talk about this on our regular podcast episode this Sunday, but I figured it would make sense to talk with the demographic this mascot is geared towards. So I sat down for a quick conversation with my 6-year old daughter.
We talk about Clark the Cub, mascots in general, her thoughts on Wrigley Field and her favorite player. If you have kids, I am interested in hearing what their thoughts are on Clark the Cub. I'd also like to hear your opinions. Feel free to leave a comment here on the site or on our Facebook page under the status update with this link.
