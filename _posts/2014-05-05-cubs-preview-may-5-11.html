What happened last week: Split with Cincinnati 1-1, Beat St. Louis 2-1; Overall 11-18 overall, 5th place in NL Central

What’s up this week: May 5-6 vs. White Sox, May 7-8 at White Sox, May 9-11 at Atlanta

Who’s pitching: Monday (Jose Quintana 1-2, 4.00 ERA vs. Jeff Samardzija 0-3, 1.98 ERA), Tuesday (Hecton Noesi 0-2, 11.12 ERA vs. Edwin Jackson 2-2, 5.24 ERA), Wednesday (Travis Wood 2-3, 3.35 ERA vs. John Danks 2-2, 5.00 ERA), Thursday (Jake Arietta 0-0, 0.00 ERA vs. Scott Carroll 1-1, 0.68 ERA), Friday (Jason Hammel 4-1, 2.43 ERA vs. Julio Teheran 2-2, 1.80 ERA), Saturday (Jeff Samardzija 0-3, 1.98 ERA vs. Ervin Santana 3-0, 2.41 ERA), Sunday (Edwin Jackson 2-2, 5.24 ERA vs. Alex Wood 2-4, 3.00 ERA)

Who to watch for: White Sox rookie first baseman Jose Abreu leads the American League with 12 home runs and 34 RBI. Jeff Samardzija is at his best in May. For his career he is 6-5 with a 2.89 ERA, 1.118 WHIP and 91 strikeouts in 90 1/3 innings.

Coming and going: May 3: activated right-handed pitcher Jake Arrieta from the 15-day disabled list and selected the contract of outfielder Chris Coghlan Triple-A Iowa. Placed Outfielder Ryan Sweeney on the 15-day disabled list and optioned left-handed pitcher Zac Rosscup to Triple-A. Transferred right-handed pitcher Kyuji Fujikawa to the 60-day disabled list.
