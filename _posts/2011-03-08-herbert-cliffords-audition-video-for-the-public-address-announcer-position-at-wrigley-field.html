The Cubs have a job opening for the 2011 season and they have gone to their fans in search for one of the most "dream job" positions with the organization.  The Cubs teamed up with CareerBuilder.com to find the new Public Address Announcer at Wrigley Field.  The submission deadline has passed and nearly 3000 people sent their resumes in and uploaded their audition videos to Youtube in hopes of becoming "The Voice of Wrigley".

A reader of the blog and listener of the podcast e-mailed me and shared his submission for this position with me.  I asked him if I could share this video with the rest of our readers and he gave me permission.  I introduce to you, Herbert Clifford.
