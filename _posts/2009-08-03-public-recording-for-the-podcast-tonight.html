We will be recording for the podcast tonight and if you are in the Quad Cities, we'd like to invite you down to Twenty Sports Bar in Moline.  We've mentioned Twenty many times on here and the podcast as the best place in the QC to watch the Cubs.

Twenty is now a sponsor for the podcast and we will be doing a test run down there tonight to get a feel for recording at the place and we will eventually do some live shows down there where you can call in and gripe about things with us.

So head to downtown Moline, drink some beer and take a Cubs pint glass home with you.  That's how they roll at Twenty.  We'll watch some of the Cubs game...record a show...and then hang out some more.
