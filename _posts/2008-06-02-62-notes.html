*  What a homestand!  The Cubs went 7-0.  What impresses me most about the Cubs sweeping the Dodgers and the Rockies is that all 7 games were tight baseball games.  This team continues to show that they can win close games and come from behind. 

*  Jim Edmonds came alive for a few games.  For the Rockies series, Edmonds went 5-7 with a double, a triple and a homerun.  He knocked in 5 RBI's.  What is more impressive is that he drew a walk and did not strikeout.  He has raised his season batting average about 45 points and is actually near .200.   Since joining the Cubs, his batting average is .258 and his on-base percentage is .303.

*  I'm glad the Cubs are hitting the road.  They have obviously proven they can win at home, going 26-8 at Wrigley.  They are 10-13 on the road and I'd like to see if they can remedy that.  I won't be looking forward to games starting at 9pm, but with a 3 week old baby, staying up late shouldn't be a problem.
