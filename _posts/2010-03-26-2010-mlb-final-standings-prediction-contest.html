The 2010 baseball season is just a few meaningless Spring Training games away.  So it's time to throw your predictions out there.  All you have to do is predict the final standings for each division and e-mail them to us: podcast@ivyenvy.com or you can just leave a comment on here with your standings.  Feel free to throw in the playoff matchups, Cy Young winners, Rookies of the Year, or anything else.

We aren't sure of the prize that will be awarded to the winner, yet.  At worst, it will be an Ivy Envy t-shirt.  At best, it will be something better than an Ivy Envy t-shirt.
