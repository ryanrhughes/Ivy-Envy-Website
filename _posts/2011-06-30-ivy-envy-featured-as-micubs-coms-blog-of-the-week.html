A few podcast episodes ago, I mentioned a new Cubs blog, miCubs.com.  miCubs is run by Brian Bedo and Jordan Bernfield, who used to do the BSquared Show.  Their site is a great resource for anyone that's interested in the Cubs' complete organization.  Brian has worked in the minor league system and has a great understanding and superb insight into the farm system.

This week, Ivy Envy was featured as miCubs.com's Blog of the Week.  Brian did an audio interview with me and posted it, along with a write-up about our site.  Go check out the article/interview here, and add their site to your list of Cubs sites to frequent.
