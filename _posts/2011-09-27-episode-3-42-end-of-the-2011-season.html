We wrap up the 2011 season with some of your responses from a few questions we posed to you. It was a pretty eventful season for the Cubs and looking ahead to the 2012 season, there are a lot of aspects of the organization up in the air.

This episode is the last of our regular season episodes. Unless a major story breaks, we will be taking a week off from recording the podcast. The off-season episodes will be shorter than the regular season episodes and they'll be recorded and released Tuesday nights.

We'd like to thank you, the loyal listeners, for sticking with us through a painful 2011 Cubs season. You made the season more enjoyable for us.
