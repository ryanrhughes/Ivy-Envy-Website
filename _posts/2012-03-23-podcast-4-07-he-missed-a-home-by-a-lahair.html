We are reaching the dog days of Spring Training, but there are some bright spots and not-so-bright spots to be paying attention to here in the last few weeks of Spring Training.

Jeff Samardzija seems to have solidified his spot in the starting rotation and we discuss who will get the other spot, who will end up pitching long-relief and who will be heading to Des Moines. The Cubs will soon announce the Opening Day starter and we give our opinions on who that person should be.

One of the few spots that are open to competition this Spring is the back-up catcher spot and we take a look at how Welington Castillo and Steve Clevenger are doing.

We also look at the players that are struggling this spring - Travis Wood, Bryan LaHair and David DeJesus. LaHair is the most interesting because of Anthony Rizzo breathing down his neck for that 1st base spot.

Then we get Joe Martin on the phone from Arizona and he tells us about a few games that he went to in the last week. Finally, we go through the captions for this week's photo. We had a lot of entries, so thanks to everyone for giving us your captions. You can join in these weekly photo caption contests over at our Facebook page.
