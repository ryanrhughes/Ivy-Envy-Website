The long winter months have passed and the season of Spring can officially begin because Opening Day is here.  Forget about last year's frustrations and this year's expectations.  It's baseball, just enjoy it.  Crack open a cold one and wait until Wednesday to live and die with every game, critique every mis-play and question every move.  There will be 161 opportunities for that other stuff.

Go Cubs.
