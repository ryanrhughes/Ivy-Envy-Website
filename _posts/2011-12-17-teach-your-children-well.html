The last page of my son's Chicago Cubs ABC book.  It's important to teach your children now what they should expect from Soriano since they will be adults when his contract comes off the books.  "Son, when this guy comes to the plate, you can expect a gaping black-hole of a strike zone and plenty of zeroes (on the scoreboard).  Also, be on the lookout for those zigzaggers heading for the infield."
