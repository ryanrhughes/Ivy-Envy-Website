Andy, Bob from Moline and Corey start things off by welcoming Brian Bedo from miCubs.com on to talk about Brett Jackson and Josh Vitters getting called up to the Cubs today. MiCubs was on the front edge of the news and always do a great job of covering the Cubs and their entire system. Brian gives a very good explanation of why the Cubs may have brought Brett Jackson up right now, even though he has been struggling in AAA, especially with strikeouts.

Then we look at all of the trades the Cubs have made in the last week and give you a little information on all the players that the Cubs acquired in the trades. We look at some listener suggested topics and questions and then pick a winner for this week's photo caption contest.
