It is becoming pretty clear to us that we will have a difficult time blogging about everything we are experiencing here in Mesa.  If you read the blog, I'd encourage you to play the podcast episodes that we are posting.  We will try to keep them short and concise for you.

Our first day exceeded our expectations in a huge way.  Some incredible seats led to a great view of the game and some interaction with the players.  If you want seats here in Mesa with a great view and player interaction, check out SpringTrainingExperience.com

Listen to Episode 3.08 to hear:
* Overall description of the Spring Training vibe.
* Recap of Sunday's walk-off win.
* Kurt's story of snagging Mike Fontenot's errant throw into the stands in the 1st inning.
* Marlon Byrd's dedication to the fans.
* Interaction with the players after the game.

Today's events are a little up-in-the-air.  They are calling for rain here in the Valley of the Sun.  After today, the weather looks great though.  The forecast says 72-75 degrees and sunny for the rest of our stay out here.

We will be posting lots of photos and doing podcasts every evening, but written descriptions of our experiences will be sparse.  And of course, if you want immediate updates from us, follow us on Twitter - @IvyEnvy.
