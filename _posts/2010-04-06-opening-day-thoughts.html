In retrospect, I should not have worried so much about this game being blacked out in our area.  I’d like to hear from folks if they had any trouble getting the game because of MLB/ESPN Blackout.  I have DirecTV and it was offered as an alternate ESPN station.

I am trying to convince myself that I should not panic over this game.  Throughout the game, I could hear myself uttering the same statement that I did for most of the season last year – “It’s ok, they’re just slumping.  They’ll come around and perform like they’re capable of.”  Now, of course I understand how absurd it is to put so much into one game.  But this thing was a train-wreck.  So I am spending today convincing myself that it was just one bad start by Zambrano, one bad relief appearance by Jeff Samardzija (hey now...careful), and a few pathetic plate appearances by everyone offensively, except Fukudome, Byrd and Ramirez.  The off-day on the second day of the season is brutal on a fan whose team fared poorly on Opening Day.

I may lose my cool in a few days if the Pirates are still ahead of the Cubs in the standings, though.
