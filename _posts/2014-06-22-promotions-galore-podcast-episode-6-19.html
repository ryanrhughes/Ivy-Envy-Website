As they hit the middle of the minor league season, the Cubs promoted some of their top prospects, including the "will never struggle in AA" and Southern League Home Run Derby Champion, Kris Bryant.

On this week's episode, Andy joins Corey in studio to discuss all of the promotions. Kyle Schwarber was promoted to Kane County after a handful of games in Short-Season Boise. Arodys Vizcaino and Armando Rivero were also promoted in the last week.

Jeff Samardzija recently rejected the most recent (and probably final) contract offer from the Cubs. We talk a little about this, and the seemingly inevitable trade that will send Samardzija to another team.

We read some listener questions and a few Internet Muscle Flexes.
