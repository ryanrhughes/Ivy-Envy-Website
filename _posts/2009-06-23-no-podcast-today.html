We had planned to get together tonight for a podcast episode but the weather looked fierce and since we record outside, we decided to cancel it.  Of course, no rain came and everything just blew around a lot.

The plan is to have one this upcoming weekend.

If you haven't heard our podcast, give it a try.  We try to keep it to 45 minutes and it's just fans talking about the Cubs.  You can listen to it here on the site or you can download it for free on iTunes.  If you listen to the podcast and like it, tell your friends that are Cubs fans.  We'd also like to hear your opinions since we throw ours around so freely.  Leave comments and get involved with the discussion.
