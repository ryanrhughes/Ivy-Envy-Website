Jeff Samardzija's bad luck/bad team continues. He's now got an 0-3 record after 8 starts despite a 1.45 ERA. This situation with Samardzija has led to the discussion of wins/losses stats for pitchers. We weigh in on this and agree that the record doesn't indicate how good the pitcher is, but it does matter for other reasons.

We dip down into the minor leagues for a while. Chris Rusin threw a no-hitter for Iowa, Jorge Soler is back and Kris Bryant is a beast. We answer several listener submitted questions, give a few scary stats and pick a winner for this week's caption contest.
