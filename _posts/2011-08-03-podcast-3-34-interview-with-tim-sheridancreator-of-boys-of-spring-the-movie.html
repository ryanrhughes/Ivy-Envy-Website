When Kurt and I were at Spring Training in March, we met with Tim Sheridan, the public address announcer for the Cubs Spring Training games at Hohokam Stadium. Tim also runs Boys of Spring, a fantastic website for Spring Training reports and resources, as well as a place for year-round analysis of the Chicago Cubs.

In this episode, we welcome Tim back on the Podcast to talk about a project he's been working on for nearly 30 years, Boys of Spring - The Movie. This film will be unlike any Cubs documentary you've seen before. Tim has been carrying around a camera for all of his years as the public address announcer at Hohokam and has captured some incredible Cubs moments over the years.
