The last thing I wanted to do this morning was climb up a cold aluminum ladder to pull down my "W" flag.  But there were points last year that I'm sure I jinxed the Cubs by being lazy and leaving it up.  That, single-handedly, led to the few Cubs losing streaks...I'm sure of it.

What we saw last night could become a reoccurring theme in 2009.  On the nights that the Cubs have difficulty generating offense (that seems to be games that Dempster pitches on the road), the bullpen will hurt us.  It's too early to criticize Kevin Gregg.   I keep trying to tell myself that about Fukudome, too.  If the Cubs can have the patience to let him hang himself, I should be able to do the same.  I'm just glad that the Cubs are deep enough to allow him the adequate time to either prove himself or quietly go away.

It's early.  I won't allow myself to look at stats or numbers until next Monday.  All I know is that I'd be more than happy to climb up that cold aluminum ladder tomorrow morning.
