This episode is the first of our weekly live video broadcasts. From here on out, you can go to www.ivyenvy.com and click on the "Live Broadcast" button on the page and watch a video stream of our podcast recording.

On this episode, we talk about the first game of the 2012 season. Will there ever be a time when we as Cubs fans couldn't fill an entire episode with complaints about the bullpen? Ryan Dempster has an incredible performance wiped away by Kerry Wood and Carlos Marmol.

Since this is the first episode of the 2012 season, we look at some statistical categories and predict the over/under on the results. Marmol saves, bullpen blown saves, LaHair and Soriano HRs, Castro errors, and dates on call-ups and trades.

Then we end the show with the weekly photo caption contest and pick a winner for the book , which is signed by radio voice of the Chicago Cubs, Pat Hughes, and co-author Rich Wolfe.
