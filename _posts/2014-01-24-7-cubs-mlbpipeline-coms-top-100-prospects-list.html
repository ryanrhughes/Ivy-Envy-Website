MLBPipleine.com revealed their Top 100 Prospects Thursday night on the MLB Network.  As expected, the Cubs organization had a strong presence in the list. There were seven Cubs prospects in the Top 100. That ranks the Cubs 2nd, only to the Red Sox in the total number of prospects listed.

Here are the rankings of Cubs players in this list:

7. Javier Baez
9. Kris Bryant
18. Albert Almora
42. C.J. Edwards
49. Jorge Soler
89. Arismendy Alcantara
100. Pierce Johnson

Prospect lists and organizational rankings have been trickling in and it's nothing but encouraging news for Cubs fans. 2014 will be a really interesting season, though. As these prospects move up to the highest levels of the minor leagues, we will have a better idea of where they are in their development.
