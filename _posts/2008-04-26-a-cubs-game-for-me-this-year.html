My wife and I are going to see the Cubs on May 11th! They are playing the Diamondbacks, who are in first in the NL West, so it should be a good matchup. I plan on taking plenty of pictures and sharing them here.

Did anyone else notice how badly Howry seemed to miss his spot on the home run pitch to Nieves?   It looked like the catcher was set up low and inside, and the pitch ended up high and outside. The catch by Johnson in the 5th was incredible though. I was just thinking yesterday about how many games I have watched this year without seeing a play like that.

I was noticing on Sports Center how when they show someone being ejected from a game by an ump, they mention a "fantasy ump league." I always thought they were joking, but there is a guy on the web who keeps track of the fantasy umpire standings.  Here is the link: Umpire Fantasy League.  If you scroll down, you can see the standings.  I'm going to go with Tim McClelland as my fantasy umpire, based soley on his morose mugshot on MLB.com's umpire bio site.  Add a comment with your pick for the Umpire Fantasy League.

Correct me if I'm wrong, but I believe the only ejection we've seen in a Cubs game was Phillies manager Charlie Manuel getting tossed when DeRosa hit that borderline foul homer against the Phillies. I hope Lou pads an ump's stats sometime soon.
