Since I can't seem to embed this crap, here is a link:  Asshat of the Week.

I think that every sports team has its share of embarassing fans.  We find ourselves wishing that these people would show some maturity, respect, and moderation.  Cards fans everywhere should be wishing that this guy would shut his hole.  It is always tough to see a longstanding member of your team playing for the enemy.  But to hate them "as a human being?"  Edmonds is probably happy to just being playing at this point and to be still making a living.  This scenario is reminiscent of being a fan of a particular band and then hanging a scarlet "S" for "sellout" around their necks when one of their songs shows up in a commecial.  Sure, there are those players like Ernie Banks who love the city they play for with great abandon, but I'm guessing a large majority of the  players out there are just happy to be making money doing what they love regardless of locale.

On the topic of embarassing fans-- Many people seem to think that the Cubs have some of the worst fans in baseball, including our friends at FOH.  I already stated my position on this issue above.  I'd be interested to hear what some of our readers think.
