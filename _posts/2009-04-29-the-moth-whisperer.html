Artemis, Aslan, Gandalf, and Ted Lilly.  All of them can commune with wild animals, but only one has the ability to throw a cliff-hanging curve ball and plow hapless catchers trying to guard the plate.  In last night's game against the Diamondbacks, Ted Lilly caught a moth and surreptitiously placed it on Gerald Perry's hat.  So add talking to animals to the prodigious list of skills that Ted possesses--check the TLFC for more info on all things TL.

I'm so glad I missed most of today's game due to work.  As my grandpa would say, "Nelly, bar the door!"  Throw some strikes, and hit the damn ball.
